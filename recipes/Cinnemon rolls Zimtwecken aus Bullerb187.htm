Title: Cinnemon rolls (Zimtwecken aus Bullerbü)

Yield: 30 rolls

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Preparation time: 2 hours

Category: Baking

Cuisine: Swedish

Source: Olaf

Ingredients

  • Yeast dough:
    • 500 g flour
    • 1/4 l milk
    • 75 g sugar
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp cardamom
    • 75 g butter
    • yeast
  • Filling:
    • 60 g butter
    • 75 g sugar
    • 3 tsp vanilla sugar
    • 1 1/2 tbsp cinnamon
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tbsp milk

Instructions

Prepare the yeast dough in your preferred way. Prepare the filling (you may want to soften the butter in the microwave oven).

Roll out to about 50x40 cm. Spread the filling on top and roll up from the long side.

Cut into 20 slices and place them onto a baking sheet that you have greased with butter. Mix an egg yolk with some milk and brush the top of the rolls with that.

Now switch on your oven and set to 250 deg C (this will give your rolls time to rise). Once this temperature has been reached, bake the rolls for about 8 minutes.
