Title: Bami Goreng

Yield: 4 portions

Category: Main

Cuisine: Indonesian

Source: Unser Kochbuch No 1, p. 328

Ingredients

  • 300 g white cabbage
  • 1 small celeriac
  • 1 leek
  • 1 onion
  • 500 g pork, or quorn pieces
  • cooking oil
  • 300 g frozen peas
  • 250 g soy vermicelli (Glasnudeln)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 tsp chilli flakes, or mild fresh red chilli
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

Cut the cabbage into very thin strips, the celeriac into little cubes, the leek into thin rings. Finely chop the onion and the chilli (if using).

Cut the meat (or quorn) into 2cm wide cubes and brown it in the oil in a large pot. Add some sugar, rice vinegar, and soy sauce, cover and keep warm on low heat.

Stir fry the vegetables for a few minutes in a separate pot. Then close and stew for 20 minutes. Add the peas about 10 minutes into the cooking time.

Soak the soy vermicelli in cold water. Then drain and cook in boiling water for 3-4 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water. Add with the meat to the vegetables.

Season with the spices and the soy sauce.
