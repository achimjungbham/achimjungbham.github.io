Title: Sausage, Spinach, Lime, and Black Bean Stew

Serves: 4

Cooking time: 40 minutes

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Mexican

Source: Georgina Hayden (The Guardian)

Rating: 5/5 stars

Ingredients

  • Sausages:
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 8 sausages (any you like; Toulouse work well)
  • Aromatics:
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and finely chopped
    • 1 red onion, peeled and finely chopped
    • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Vegetables:
    • 500 g fresh spinach
    • 400 g tinned black beans, including juices
    • 400 g tinned chopped tomatoes
  • Spices:
    • 1 tsp sweet smoked paprika
    • 2 tsp dark brown sugar
    • juice of 1 lime
    • 3/4 tsp salt
    • black pepper
  • Garnish and sides:
    • fresh coriander, finely chopped
    • 300 ml sour cream
    • 8 corn tortillas (or plain rice)

Instructions

Put a wide sauté pan or large frying pan on a medium heat and drizzle in a tablespoon of olive oil. Fry the sausages for about eight minutes in total, until nicely browned all over, then transfer to a plate.

Add another tablespoon of olive oil and sauté the aromatics for about 2 or 3 minutes.

Add the spinach and a splash of water and cook until wilted.

Add the vegetables and spices and bring to a boil.

Position the sausages on top and transfer the pan (uncovered) to the oven. Set the oven to 200℃ (fan assisted) and cook for 20 minutes.

Scatter the coriander over the top and serve with tortillas (or rice) together with the sour cream.
