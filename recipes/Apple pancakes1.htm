Title: Apple pancakes

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: German; vegetarian

Source: Louise Harrer

Ingredients

  • 250 g flour
  • 400 ml milk
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 500 g apples
  • oil
  • icing sugar; or sugar-cinnamon mixture

Instructions

Mix flour, eggs, and salt with half the milk and stir well to make a smooth dough. Add the remaining milk to get a fairly runny batter. Leave to stand. Put frying pan on stove and preheat.

Cut apples into a small pieces and add to the batter.

Fry in small batches in plenty of oil.

Serve with plenty of icing sugar on top, or with sugar-cinnamon mixture.
