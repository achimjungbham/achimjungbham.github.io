Title: Fruit Compote

Yield: 1 litre

Preparation time: 40 minutes

Cooking time: 3-5 minutes

Category: Preserves

Source: Luise Haarer, p.141

Ingredients

  • Apple compote
    • 750 g peeled and cored apples
    • 500 ml water
    • 75-100 g sugar
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • Cooking time: 3.5 minutes (approx.)
  • Plum compote
    • 750 g de-stoned plums
    • 250 ml water
    • 60 g sugar
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • Cooking time: 5 minutes (approx.)
  • Apricote compote
    • 750 g de-stoned apricotes
    • 250 ml water
    • 60 g sugar
    • Cooking time: 5 minutes (approx.)

Instructions

Bring the appropriate amount of water together with the sugar and the cinnamon stick (if using) to a boil. Let it simmer (with the lid on) while you prepare the fruit.

When you have the amount of fruit required, put in a large pot and pour over the hot sugar water. Bring to a rapid boil and cook for the appropriate time (about 3.5 minutes for apples, 5 minutes for plums/apricots). Don't allow the fruit pieces to disintegrate completely.

Fill immediately into clean preserving containers, put on the lids, and let them stand upside-down for a while to sterilize the lids.

Note: All quantities and cooking times are approximate as they depend on the variety of fruit available.
