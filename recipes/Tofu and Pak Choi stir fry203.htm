Title: Tofu and Pak Choi stir fry

Yield: 2 portions

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Tibetan

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Taste Tibet, p.89

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 300 g firm tofu
  • 1 mild red chilli
  • 2 large garlic cloves
  • 400 g pak choi
  • 2 tsp light soy sauce
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp Chinese rice vinegar
  • salt

Instructions

Start a pot of rice (from one cup of rice).

Cut the tofu into bite size pieces. Slice the garlic and chilli thinly. Wash and cut the pak choi.

Heat the oil in a frying pan or wok. Carefully fry the tofu from at least two sides (takes about 5 min each side). Add garlic and chilli half-way through. Remove all from pan when the tofu is nicely browned.

Add more oil if necessary and add the (still wet) pak choi. Cook for about 5 min until wilted.

Combine everything and season with soy sauce, vinegar, salt and pepper.

Serve with rice.
