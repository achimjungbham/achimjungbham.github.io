Title: Roasted Paneer Aloo Gobi

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Main

Cuisine: Indian; vegetarian

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Meera Sodha, East

Ingredients

  • Marinade:
    • 100 ml sunflower oil
    • 2 1/2 tsp ground coriander
    • 2 1/2 tsp ground cumin
    • 2 sp Kashmiri chilli powder
    • 1/2 tsp garam masala
    • 1 1/2 tsp salt
    • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
    • 2 cm ginger, peeled and grated
  • Vegetables I:
    • 1 medium cauliflower
    • 300 g potatoes (raw or pre-cooked)
  • Vegetables II:
    • 2 red onions
    • 250 g paneer
  • Vegetables III:
    • 500 g cherry tomatoes
  • Garnish:
    • fresh coriander (optional)
  • Side:
    • rice or Indian bread

Instructions

Pre-heat oven to 210 deg Celsius fan (or 230 deg non-fan). Brush a baking sheet with oil. Start a pot of rice.

Mix the ingredients for the marinade.

Break cauliflower into bit-sized florets. Peel the potatoes and cut into small cubes (or larger cubes if using pre-cooked ones). Mix thoroughly with half the marinade and spread on the baking sheet. Bake for 15 minutes.

In the meantime, cut the paneer into bite-sized cubes, and the onions into thick wedges. Mix with the remaining half of the marinade and add onto the baking sheet. Reduce heat to 190 deg and bake for another 10 minutes.

Meanwhile, cut the tomatoes in half, mix with a little salt. Add to the baking sheet and bake for a final 5 minutes.

Allow to rest and cool a little, then scrape off the baking sheet into a serving bowl and sprinkle some coriander on top.

Serve with the rice or with Indian bread.
