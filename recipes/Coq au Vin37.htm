Title: Coq au Vin

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 1 hour

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Entree

Cuisine: French

Source: Classic Home Cooking, p.159

Ingredients

  • 2 tblsp butter
  • 1 tblsp sunflower oil
  • 1 1/2 kg chicken -- cut into pieces
  • 4 slices bacon
  • 8 shallots
  • 250 g mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 300 ml chicken stock
  • 300 ml red wine
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 sprig thyme
  • 1 sprig parsley
  • 1 clove garlic
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

Pre-heat oven to 180 C.
Melt butter and oil in a casserole.
Cook the chicken pieces for 10-12 minutes in the butter and oil. Lift out and drain.
Add bacon, shallots and mushrooms and cook until golden brown. Lift out and drain.
Add flour and cook for 3-5 minutes until lightly browned. Gradually pour in stock, then the wine, stirring until smooth.
Return the chicken, bacon and the rest, and add herbs. Season to taste.
Bring to a boil, cover and cook for 45-60 minutes in the oven.
