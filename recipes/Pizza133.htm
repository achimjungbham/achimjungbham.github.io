Title: Pizza

Serves: 2

Preparation time: 10+40+60 minutes

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Italian

Ingredients

  • Base
    • 160 ml lukewarm water
    • 1/2 tsp dried yeast
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 2 tbsp semolina
    • Pizza flour (type 00)
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • Some more semolina to roll out the dough
  • Tomato sauce
    • 150 ml tomato passata
    • 1 tbsp concentrated tomato paste
    • 1 clove garlic
    • 1 tsp paprika
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • Toppings
    • 150 g Gouda cheese, grated
    • 1 ball mozarella, cut into about 8 slices
    • 2 tsp dried oregano
    • 150 g salami slices
    • 1 small jar mushrooms, sliced
    • 1 small jar artichoke hearts, sliced (optional)

Instructions

Prepare the yeast dough in your preferred way and allow it to rise for at least an hour (the longer the better). In the meantime:

Mix well the ingredients for the tomato sauce. Check the seasoning.

Prepare the toppings.

There will be enough dough for two small or one large pizza. Roll out the dough using the extra semolina to stop it sticking to the worktop. Place on baking sheets greased with olive oil. Spread the tomato sauce on top, then the two cheeses. Sprinkle with the oregano and add salami and mushrooms.

Bake in a very hot oven for about 15 minutes.
