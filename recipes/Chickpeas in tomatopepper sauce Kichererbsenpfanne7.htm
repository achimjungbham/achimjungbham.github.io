Title: Chickpeas in tomato-pepper sauce (Kichererbsenpfanne)

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: American; vegetarian

Source: Three Rivers Cookbook, p.115

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 stalk leek; or one medium onion
  • 2 peppers; red and green
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin chickpeas; or kidney beans
  • black pepper
  • salt
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 200 g smoked ham; or chorizo sausage
  • 20 olives; black in brine (optional)

Instructions

Start the rice.

Thinly slice leek and peppers. Place in a large pan with olive oil and fry gently until soft. Add tomatoes, crushed garlic, salt, pepper, and paprika. Add olives if desired. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes. Add ham before serving with rice.
