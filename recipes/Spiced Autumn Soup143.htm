Title: Spiced Autumn Soup

Source: Mary Berry Classic Home Cooking, p. 29

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp ghee or butter
  • 2 large onions, coarsely chopped
  • 2 potatoes, coarsely chopped
  • 2 carrots, coarsely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, crushed
  • zest and juice from 1 orange
  • 2 tsp mild curry powder
  • 1 litre chicken or vegetable stock
  • 2 cans chopped tomatoes
  • 2 apples, core removed
  • 1 tbsp dried basil
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp mild chilli powder (optional)

Instructions

Prepare all ingredients. Melt butter/ghee in a large sauce pan, add the onions and saut√© until translucent. Add potatoes, carrots, and garlic and fry for about 5 minutes more. Add the curry powder and cook, stirring constantly. Add all other ingredients and bring to a boil. Cook on low heat for about 45 minutes.

Puree the soup with a hand blender, check for seasoning, and serve.

Notes

Potatoes and carrots can be replaced with sweet potatoes and/or butternut squash.
