Title: Italian Meat Balls (à la Paola)

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 45 minutes

Preparation time: 25 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Italian

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Paola

Ingredients

  • Meat balls:
    • 500 g minced pork
    • zest of one lemon
    • 1 egg
    • 40 g parmesan, grated
    • 2 slices white bread, cut into very small pieces
    • 4 tbsp milk
    • 1 tsp salt
    • pepper
    • nutmeg
    • olive oil for frying
  • Sauce:
    • 1 red pepper
    • 1 yellow pepper
    • 1 can chopped tomatoes
    • 2 onions, chopped
    • 1 tsp salt
    • pepper

Instructions

Prepare all ingredients. Add milk to the bread crumbs. Mix all ingredients for the meat balls, form smallish balls and fry them in a non-stick pan until all sides are light brown. Set aside.

Sauté the onions in the same pan until translucent.

In a blender, blitz the tomatoes and peppers. Season to taste.

In a pan for which you have a good lid, put everything together, bring to a boil, and simmer for 45 minutes.
