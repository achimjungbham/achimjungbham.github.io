Title: Pork loin medallions

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Category: Meat

Cuisine: German

Ingredients

  • 1 pork loin
  • sunflower oil
  • 100 ml stock; chicken or pork
  • 200 ml single cream
  • 2 tsp green peppercorns
  • pepper and salt
  • 1 drop Worcester sauce

Instructions

Pre-heat oven to 80 deg C. Put oil in a frying pan and put on full heat. Prepare stock.

Slice pork loin into 2cm thick medallions. Brush with oil on all sides and sprinkle with salt and pepper.

Fry medallions in hot pan for 3 minutes on each side (not longer). Take out, cover, and put into oven.

Add stock and green peppercorns to pan and bring to a boil. Cook for a little while and add cream. Season to taste and add a drop or two of Worcester sauce.

Arrange medallions on a serving plate and pour gravy over them, or serve gravy separately.

Notes

Nice with potatoes and glazed carrots.
