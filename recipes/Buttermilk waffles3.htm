Title: Buttermilk waffles

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: German; vegetarian

Source: Zwergenst√ºbchen Backbuch; p. 17

Ingredients

  • 125 g butter
  • 50 g sugar
  • 2 tblsp vanilla sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 4 eggs
  • 250 g flour
  • 1 tsp backing powder
  • 200 ml buttermilk
  • oil
  • 250 g quark (optional)
  • raspberries; or cherries from a tin (optional)

Instructions

Mix with a hand-mixer the soft butter, sugar, vanilla sugar, salt, and eggs. Sift flour with backing powder. Add one spoonful at a time and carefully work in.

Add buttermilk to obtain a fairly runny batter.

Heat waffle iron, brush with oil and start cooking your waffles.

Serve with raspberry quark, for example.
