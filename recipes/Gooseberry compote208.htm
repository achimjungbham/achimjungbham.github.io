Title: Gooseberry compote

Yield: 3 litres

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Preserves

Cuisine: French

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Unser Kochbuch No 1, p. 435

Ingredients

  • 2 kg gooseberries
  • 1/2 l white wine
  • 1 kg sugar
  • 1 stick cinnamon
  • 2 cloves

Instructions

Day one: Trim the gooseberries of their stems and flowerheads. Wash thoroughly. Meanwhile, boil the wine, sugar, cinnamon, and cloves for 10 minutes. Pour the boiling hot syrup over the gooseberries in a pot for which you have a tight-closing lid. Leave for 24 hours.

Day two, three, and four: Drain the syrup from the gooseberries, re-heat and pour over the berries. Leave for another 24 hours.

Day five: Bring syrup WITH berries to a boil and fill into preserving containers.
