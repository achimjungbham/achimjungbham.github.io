Title: Corn on the cob

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Preparation time: 5 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: American; vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 4 cob corn
  • salted butter

Instructions

Place corn in pressure cooker with half a cup of water. Close lid and pressurize. Cook at high pressure for 5 minutes. Leave to cool.

In the meantime, prepare your rice.

Notes

If you don't have salted butter, sprinkle a bit of salt over the buttered corn.
