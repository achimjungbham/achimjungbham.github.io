Title: Swiss roll (Biskuitrolle)

Yield: 1 cake

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Baking

Cuisine: German

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Dr Oetker

Original Page from www.oetker.de

Ingredients

  • Filling:
    • 250 ml whipping cream
    • 1 package Sahnesteif or 1 tbsp starch
    • 250 g quark
    • 1 tbsp lemon juice
    • 50 g sugar
    • 6 leaves gelatine
    • 250 g strawberries or raspberries
  • Dough:
    • 4 eggs, separated
    • 1 additional egg yolk
    • 70 g sugar
    • 1 package vanilla sugar
    • 80 g flour
    • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Decoration:
    • 1 tbsp icing sugar

Instructions

Soak the gelative leaves in cold water. If you are using strawberries, wash them thoroughly and cut into cubes the size of dice.

Whip the cream until it forms soft peaks. Add the Sahnesteif (or starch) and beat a little more.

Thoroughly mix quark, lemon juice, and sugar.

Carefully heat the softened gelatine leaves (be careful not to bring to boiling point), and divide evenly between cream and quark mixture. Mix well, then combine the two mixtures. Add the fruit, mix again, and put in fridge.

Pre-heat oven to 200 deg Celsius (or 180 deg fan-assisted).

Line a baking sheet with non-stick baking paper, using a bit of butter to keep it in place.

Beat the egg whites until stiff. Add yolks and sugar and beat until you have a smooth cream colored mixture. Mix the flour with the baking powder and carefully fold into egg-sugar mixture. Spread evenly on the baking sheet and put in the hot oven for 10 minutes.

In the meantime, lay out a clean kitchen towel and spread about 2 tbsp caster sugar on it.

When the 10 minutes are up or the biscuit has a nice light brown colour on top, take it out and immediately flip it onto the sugared towel.

Soften the baking paper with cold water and carefully remove it from the cake. Spread the cream-quark filling evenly on the cake and roll up immediately with the help of the towel. Place the roll onto a suitable cake tray and put it into the fridge to set fully.

Before serving, decorate with icing sugar.
