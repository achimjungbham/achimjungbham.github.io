Title: Oat cakes

Yield: 8 pancakes

Cooking time: 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 2 hours

Category: Main

Cuisine: English

Source: Metro Newspaper

Ingredients

  • 225 g fine oatmeal
  • 100 g wholemeal flour
  • 100 g plain flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 10 g fresh yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 700 ml lukewarm milk and water
  • 100 g cheese (optional)
  • 1 handful chives (optional)

Instructions

Mix yeast with lukewarm milk water mixture.
Mix dry ingredients.
Stir in about 1/3 of dry ingredients into yeast liquid and set aside in a warm place for half an hour.
Stir in the remaining flour an let rise for an hour.
Bake like pan cakes.
If desired, sprinkle a pancake with cheese and chives, roll up, and put in a warm oven until ready for serving.
