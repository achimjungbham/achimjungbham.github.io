Title: Plum Cake

Category: Cake

Cuisine: German

Ingredients

  • Sweet yeast dough
  • 90 plums

Instructions

Prepare a sweet yeast dough following this recipe and line a baking sheet with it.

While the dough is rising on the sheet, de-stone the plums: Incise the ends and then cut lengthwise into two halves at 90 degrees to the initial incisions.

Lay the plum halves onto the dough: 15 rows at 12 halves each works well, see the photo.

Bake at 200℃ for about 30 minutes.
