Title: Quiche Lorraine

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Main

Cuisine: French

Source: Das neue Kochvergnuegen; p. 218

Ingredients

  • 250 g flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 125 g butter; cold
  • 250 g lardons; smoked
  • 250 ml cream
  • 3 eggs
  • 50 g gruyere
  • pepper
  • nutmeg

Instructions

Mix salt into flour. Cut butter into this (or use hand-mixer). Add 3 to 5 tblsp cold water for a smooth dough. Wrap with cling film. Leave in fridge for at least one hour.

Pre-heat oven to 200 deg C.

Put lardons in small pan and gently fry. Put on kitchen towels to drain the fat.

Brush a (non-stick) baking form oil or the lardon fat. Line with the dough.

Spread lardons on top. Mix cream, eggs, plenty of freshly ground black pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg. Don't add any salt. Grate the Gruy√®re cheese and add to the cream mixture. Pour into the pie shell.

Bake for 30 minutes.
