Title: Chocolate Almond Cherry Cake

Yield: 1

Category: Cake

Cuisine: German

Source: Zwergenst√ºbchen Backbuch; p. 69

Ingredients

  • 250 g sugar
  • 125 g butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 125 g flour
  • 125 g almonds
  • 100 g cooking chocolate
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 3 tsp baking powder
  • 1 jar morello cherries

Instructions

Pre-heat oven to 175 deg Celsius (150 fan assisted).

Grind the almonds and chop the chocolate into a smallish pieces.

Drain the cherries.

Mix sugar, softened butter, and eggs until smooth and fluffy. Then add flour, almonds, cinnamon, and baking powder, and mix carefully.

Grease a springform tin with butter and pour in the dough. Spread the cherries evenly on top and place in the oven. Bake for 40-45 minutes.
