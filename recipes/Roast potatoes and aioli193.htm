Title: Roast potatoes and aioli

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Modern; vegetarian

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Ottolenghi Test Kitchen, p 78

Ingredients

  • Roast potatoes:
    • 1 kg baby new potatoes
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • salt
    • black pepper
  • Aioli:
    • 2 cloves garlic; or 1 large one
    • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
    • 1 egg
    • 1 egg yolk
    • 100 ml olive oil
    • 100 ml sunflower oil
    • 1 tbsp lemon juice
    • 75 g Greek-style yoghurt
  • Pine nuts:
    • 1 tbsp butter
    • 40 g pine nuts
    • 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
  • Garnish:
    • 1/2 bunch parsley

Instructions

Pre-heat oven to 220 deg fan.

Cook the baby potatoes for 5 minutes in the pressure cooker. Halve them and mix them with the olive oil, 1/2 tsp salt and a good grind of pepper. Transfer to a baking sheet and bake in the hot oven for about 20 minutes, or until they are golden and brown around the edges.

Meanwhile, peel and crush the garlic into a food processor bowl, add mustard, egg, and egg yolk, and blitz for 10 seconds. Slowly add the oil, blitzing after every tablespoon full. Finally add the lemon juice and blitz once more. Transfer to a nice serving bowl and add the yoghurt, stirring well.

Melt the butter in a small pan and roast the pine nuts until they have a nice colour. Remove from heat and add paprika. Transfer to a serving bowl.

Chop the parsley and put into yet another serving bowl.

Serve everything separately.
