Title: Green pancakes and lime butter

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 1 hour

Category: Main

Cuisine: Modern

Source: Yotam Ottolenghi

Original Page from www.cuisinedaubery.com

Ingredients

  • Lime butter:
    • 120 g butter
    • 1 clove garlic
    • 1 lime; zested
    • 1 1/2 tbsp lime juice
    • 1/4 tsp salt
    • 1/2 tsp white pepper
    • 1 tbsp cilantro leaves; chopped
    • 1/4 tsp chilli flakes
  • Pancakes:
    • 250 g spinach
    • 6 green onions
    • 2 green chillies
    • 2 egg whites
    • 90 g flour
    • 1 tbsp baking powder
    • 150 ml milk
    • 1 egg
    • 60 g butter
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • olive oil

Instructions

Soften the butter in the microwave oven. Mix all ingredients for the lime butter. Tip onto cling film and roll into a sausage shape. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

Wilt the spinach leaves in a hot pan with a bit of water. Drain and chop.

Finely chop the onions and de-seeded chillies. Whisk the egg whites. Melt the butter. Mix the flour with the baking powder.

Mix together the milk with the melted butter, the egg, and the spices. Add the flour and mix well for a good pancake dough. Add onions, chillies, and spinach. Carefully fold in the egg whites.

Fry the pancakes about 2 minutes each side. Aim for a size of 7cm across and 1cm thick.

Serve with slices of the lime butter.

Notes

Save any unused butter by freezing it.
