Title: Lasagne

Yield: 4 portions

Category: Main

Cuisine: Italian; can be vegetarian

Source: Kochvergnuegen p. 205

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 6 tbsp olive oil
  • 300 g minced meat; or quorn
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cayenne pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 200 ml red wine
  • 400 g mushrooms
  • 2 cans chopped tomatoes
  • 1 bunch parsley
  • 600 g mozzarella
  • 250 g lasagne leaves
  • 150 g creme fraiche
  • 4 tbsp cream
  • 4 tbsp parmesan cheese, freshly grated

Instructions

Pre-heat oven to 200 deg Celsius.

Pour the tomatoes into a sauce pan, add the chopped parsley, and season with salt and pepper. Bring to a boil and leave to simmer uncovered while you prepare the remaining incredients.

Chop the onion and sautee for 10 minutes in 2 tbsp olive oil. Add the minced meat and brown well. Season with salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper. Add the crushed garlic and the wine. Cover and leave to stew for 10 minutes.

Clean the mushrooms and cut into slices. In a second pan, heat 2 tbsp olive oil and sautee the mushrooms for about 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.

Cut the mozarella into thin slices.

Take a large casserole dish and grease with 1 tbsp olive oil.

Place into the dish in three layers: lasagne leaves, meat, mushrooms, mozarella slices, tomato sauce.

Cover with a fourth layer of lasagne leaves.

Mix creme fraiche with cream and parmesan, and season lightly. Spread this mixture across the top layer.

Cover the dish with foil and place in the hot oven for about 30 minutes. Then uncover and bake for 10 minutes more until the surface becomes golden and crisp in places.
