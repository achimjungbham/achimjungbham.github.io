Title: Honigplätzchen

Yield: 60 biscuits

Cooking time: 12 minutes

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Category: Christmas cookies

Cuisine: German

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Chefkoch.de

Original Page from www.chefkoch.de

Ingredients

  • 175 g butter
  • 125 g brown cane sugar
  • 6 tbsp honey
  • 350 g flour
  • 4 tsp cinnemon
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 2 tsp baking powder (or Hirschhornsalz)
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 egg yolk

Instructions

In a sauce pan, melt butter, sugar, and honey.

In a separate bowl mix all remaining dry ingredients. In yet a separate cup, beat the egg.

Add the egg to the flour, mix a bit and then add the (still hot) melted butter. Knead into a dough ball (it won't be sticky). Wrap with cling film and put in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.

Prehead the oven to 180 deg C. Line two baking sheets with with grease paper. Separate one egg carefully to get a pure egg yolk.

Sprinkle flour on a work top and roll the dough to about 7 mm thickness. Use cookie cutters to cut out your biscuit shapes and place them onto the baking sheets.

Brush the tops with egg yolk and bake for 12 minutes.
