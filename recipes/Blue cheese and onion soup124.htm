Title: Blue cheese and onion soup

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 5 minutes

Category: Soup

Cuisine: French

Source: Mary Berry Classic Home Cooking

Ingredients

  • 600 ml milk
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 80 g butter
  • 2 large onions
  • 60 g flour
  • 1 1/2 litres chicken or vegetable stock
  • black pepper
  • 150 g blue cheese, for example, Stilton

Instructions

Add bay leaves and nutmeg to milk in a saucepan. Bring to a boil. Turn off heat and allow to steep for 20 minutes.

Prepare the stock. Finely slice onions.

Melt butter in a large pan and cook onions very gently for 10 minutes. Do not allow them to turn brown. Add flour and cook for 2 minutes more, stirring all the while. Gradually blend in milk, then stock. Add pepper to taste and let simmer for up to 10 minutes more.

Add the crumbled cheese and allow it to melt, but do not boil the soup. Taste for seasoning.

Serve immediately, perhaps with a little light cream added for effect.
