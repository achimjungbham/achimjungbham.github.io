Title: Meatballs (Ingrid's Fleischküchle)

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Meat

Cuisine: German

Ingredients

  • 500 g minced pork
  • 1 bread roll; stale
  • 1 bunch parsley
  • 1 small onion
  • 1/2 tsp salt; or more
  • pepper
  • 1 egg
  • breadcrumbs
  • sunflower oil

Instructions

Soak roll in water. Chop parsley and onions.

Squeeze roll to remove excess water. Mix with all ingredients except breadcrumbs.

Form small balls. Use breadcrumbs to cover.

Fry in medium hot pan in plenty of oil until brown on all sides. Cover and cook for 10 min at low heat.

Notes

Goes well with potatoes and vegetables (eg, leek). Smaller balls are nice for finger food. Leftovers are great sandwich fillers.
