Title: Mushroom and Fennel Hot Pot

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 40 minutes

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: English; vegetarian

Source: Vegetarian; p. 241

Ingredients

  • 1-2 heads fennel
  • 12 shallots
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 150 g mushrooms
  • 2 dried tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1/4 tsp dried chilli flakes
  • 1 bay leaf
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1/2 cube chicken stock
  • 300 ml dry cider
  • Bulgur:
    • 1 cup bulgur
    • 2 cups water
    • 1 tsp harissa spices

Instructions

Cut the fennel into large pieces.

Pour boiling water over the shallots, then peel them.

Heat the oil and sauté fennel and shallots for 10 minutes.

In the meantime, cut the mushrooms into large pieces. Add them to the pot and sauté for another 3 minutes.

Add all the spices and pour in the cider. Bring to a boil, then simmer gently for 30 minutes. Check for seasoning.

Put bulgur, water, salt, and harissa spices into a pot and bring to a boil. Lower heat and leave until the hot pot is ready.
