Title: Roast chicken with sweet potato

Yield: 2 portions

Cooking time: 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 25 minutes

Category: Complete meal

Cuisine: Contemporary English

Source: Tanis (Nigella Express)

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • 2 clove garlic
  • 2 chicken breast with skin
  • 1 sweet potato
  • 1/4 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 bunch watercress
  • 1 lime
  • salt and pepper; to taste

Instructions

Preheat oven to 220 degree Celsius.
Crush garlic into 4 tblsp of olive oil.
Peel potatoes and cut into 5cm cubes.

Brush 1 tblsp of garlic oil over each chicken breast.
Put potato cubes into a mixing bowl, add some of the garlic oil, cinnamon and cumin. Mix well to cover evenly.

Cook meat and potatoes in the hot oven for approx. 25 minutes.

Put each chicken breast on a plate, with a tangle of watercress and sweet potatoes alongside. Sprinkle with salt flakes (eg Maldon) and pepper to taste. Spritz with lime juice.
