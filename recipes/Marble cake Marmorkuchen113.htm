Title: Marble cake (Marmorkuchen)

Yield: 1 cake

Category: Cake

Cuisine: German

Source: Paola

Ingredients

  • 250 g butter; unsalted
  • 275 g sugar
  • 1 pack vanilla flavoured sugar
  • 5 eggs
  • 250 g flour
  • 250 g starch
  • baking powder
  • 150 ml milk
  • 3 tbsp cocoa powder; heaped tablespoons

Instructions

Whisk the butter until frothy, add sugar and vanilla, then the egg yolks one by one.

Mix flour, starch and baking powder together and add gradually to the sugar-egg mix.
Add the milk gradually at this time too, as the dough gets drier.

Whisk the egg whites until stiff and fold them gently into the dough.

Pour 2/3 of the dough into a buttered tin.

Mix the cocoa with the rest and add this on top, mixing with a fork with spiraling movements.

Bake for just over one hour (180Â° - gas mark 4), though depending on the cake tin and the oven 45-50 min can also be enough.
