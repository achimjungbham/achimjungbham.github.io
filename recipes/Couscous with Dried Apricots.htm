Title: Couscous with Dried Apricots

Serves: 3

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Cooking time: 25 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: English modern

Source: Ottolenghi, p. 80

Ingredients

  • Squash
    • 1 butternut squash, peeled, de-seeded, and cut into cubes
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • salt and pepper
  • Couscous
    • 2 cups couscous
    • 3 cups light stock
    • a pinch of saffron strands
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Additions
    • 50 g dried apricots, cut into small cubes
    • 3 tbsp chopped tarragon (or 1.5 tsp dried)
    • 3 tbsp chopped parsley
    • 3/4 tsp cinnamon
    • zest of 1/2 lemon
  • Garnish
    • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1/3 tsp salt

Instructions

Preheat oven to 180℃.

Prepare butternut squash cubes, mix with olive oil and season well. Spread on a baking sheet and bake for 25 minutes.

Prepare stock, pour over couscous and saffron, cover and allow to absorb.

Cut the apricots into cubes and cover with boiling water. Allow to soak.

Thinly slice the onion and sauté in some olive oil for about 10 minutes

Add some olive oil to the couscous and fluff up with a fork. Put in a large serving bowl, add the drained apricot cubes and butternut squash pieces, plus the herbs, cinnamon and lemon zest. Mix well but carefully. Taste and adjust seasoning if necessary. Finish by sprinkling the fried onion rings on top.

Serve warm but not hot.
