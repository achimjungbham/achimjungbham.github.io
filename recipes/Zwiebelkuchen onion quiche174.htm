Title: Zwiebelkuchen (onion quiche)

Yield: 1 cake

Cooking time: 40 minutes

Preparation time: 2 hours

Category: Main

Cuisine: German

Source: Luise Harrer; p 169

Ingredients

  • Yeast dough base:
    • 130 ml milk, lukewarm
    • 250 g flour
    • yeast
    • 1/3 tsp salt
    • 40 g sunflower oil
  • Filling:
    • 4 tbsp oil
    • 750 g onions
    • 125 g lardons, smoked
    • 250 ml sour cream
    • 50 g flour
    • 3 eggs
    • 1 tsp caraway seeds
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tsp vinegar (optional)
    • 100 g Emmental cheese (optional)

Instructions

Mix half the flour with the dried yeast and add the lukewarm milk. Mix well and put in a warm spot to prove. After about an hour, add the remaining ingredients and knead well, then allow to rise for another hour.

Meanwhile, chop the onions and cook with the lardons for about 10 minutes until the onions are translucent. You may need to add a little oil to get the lardons going. Turn down heat and allow to cool.

Pre-heat oven to 200 deg Celsius.

Line a well-greased baking time with the yeast dough.

Mix well sour cream, salt, eggs, flour, and caraway seeds. If desired, add vinegar and/or cheese. Mix with onions and lardons and pour into pie shell.

Bake for 40 - 50 minutes. (You may wish to reduce heat to 175 deg after about 30 minutes.)
