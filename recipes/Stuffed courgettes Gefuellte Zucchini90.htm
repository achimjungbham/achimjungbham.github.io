Title: Stuffed courgettes (Gefuellte Zucchini)

Yield: 4 portions

Category: Main

Cuisine: German

Source: Kochvergnuegen S. 184

Ingredients

  • 4 courgettes
  • Filling:
    • 1 bunch basil
    • 1/2 bunch parsley
    • 200 ml single cream
    • 200 g white bread or bun
    • 100 g cooked ham
    • 2 tbsp walnuts
    • 1 egg
    • salt
    • pepper
    • nutmeg
    • 450 g mozzarella
  • Sauce:
    • 200 g onions
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 125 ml chicken broth
    • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • Accompaniment:
    • 400 g tagliatelle

Instructions

Pick basil leaves off the stems and chop finely, then mix with cream. Cut the bread slices into small cubes and add to cream.

Cut courgettes lengthwise into halves. Scrape core with a spoon and chop. Set aside for later.

Cut ham into small cubes.

Chop walnuts.

Beat the egg.

Add egg, walnuts, and ham to bread-cream mixture. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Mix well.

Fill courgette halves with the mixture.

Turn on oven and set to 220 degrees Celsius.

Chop onions finely and sautee them in the olive oil. When soft, add the courgette cores and sautee a little longer. Add the broth and cook for 5 minutes. Blitz everything into a smooth sauce. Season with salt and pepper and add the vinegar.

Pour the sauce into a casserole dish and place the filled courgette halves tightly packed into it. Cover with slizes of mozzarella.

Put into hot oven and bake for 20 minutes.

Serve with tagliatelle pasta.
