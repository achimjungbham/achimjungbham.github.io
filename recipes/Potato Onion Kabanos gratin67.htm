Title: Potato Onion Kabanos gratin

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 1 hour

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Main

Cuisine: German

Source: Annette Baier

Ingredients

  • 600 g onions; sliced
  • 500 g potatoes; cut into small cubes or slices
  • 200 g Kabanos; or some other spicy dry cured sausage such as Chorizo
  • 2 tblsp oil
  • 200 ml vegetable broth
  • 200 ml soured cream
  • 2 tsp flour
  • 2 eggs
  • nutmeg
  • allspice

Instructions

Pre-heat oven to 200 deg Celsius.

Prepare ingredients: peel and slice onions, peel and cut potatoes, cut sausage into thin slices, prepare broth.

Take an oven-proof dish and brush with oil. Fill in layers: potatoes (flavoured with allspice), half the onions, half the kabanos slices, remaining onions, finally topped with the remaining kabanos slices. Pour in the broth.

Mix eggs, sour cream, nutmeg, allspice, flour and pour over the dish.

Cover with aluminium foil and put into hot oven. Cook for 40 minutes. Remove cover and cook for a further 15 minutes.

Notes

Depending on the sausage used, additional salt will not be needed.
