Title: Mulligatawny

Yield: 6 servings

Cooking time: 2 hours

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Soup

Cuisine: Indian; vegetarian

Source: Heston Blumental

Ingredients

  • 2 onions
  • 60 g butter
  • 1 tblsp tomato puree
  • 1 tblsp mild curry powder
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 tsp ground cloves
  • 1 green chilli
  • 1 red pepper
  • 3 clove garlic
  • 250 g puy lentils
  • 40 g raisins
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 2/5 l chicken stock
  • black pepper
  • plain joghurt; for serving (optional)

Instructions

Chop onions and saute in butter until soft in a large pot. Add tomato puree and curry powder and continue frying gently for 15 minutes, stirring often. Put the tinned tomatoes, finely chopped chilli, crushed garlic, chopped red pepper, ground black pepper into a blender and render to a pulp. Pour this into the cooking pot. Simmer for a while.

Wash lentils and add to pot, together with raisins and sugar. Pour in the chicken stock and bring to a boil. Cover and cook gently for an hour and a half. Add salt and more black pepper to taste.

Serve with a swirl of yoghurt into which you have mixed some chopped fresh mint (if available).
