Title: Penne with Avocado Lime Sauce

Yield: 2 portions

Category: Main

Cuisine: German; vegetarian

Source: 1 Nudel - 50 Saucen, p. 10

Ingredients

  • Avocado sauce:
    • 1 lime
    • 1 red chilli
    • 1/2 bunch parsley
    • 1 large avocado
    • 75 g sour cream
    • 75 g yoghurt
    • 1/2 tbsp olive oil
    • salt
    • 1 tsp sugar
  • Pasta:
    • 300 g pasta (150g per person)

Instructions

Grate the lime peel, then squeeze for the juice. De-seed the chilli and cut very finely. Wash parsley and chop finely, too.

Half the avocados and remove the seed (carefully). Remove flesh with a spoon.

Mix all incredients with the hand-blender and place in fridge until needed.

Cook your pasta to instruction and serve with the avocado cream.
