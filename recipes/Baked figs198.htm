Title: Baked figs

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 35 minutes

Preparation time: 2 minutes

Category: Dessert

Cuisine: English; vegetarian

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Nigel Slater, Observer

Ingredients

  • 300 g fresh figs
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • 90 ml Grand Marnier

Instructions

Set the oven at 200C. Cut the figs in half from stem to base and place them, just touching, in a baking dish. Scatter them with the sugar and pour over the orange juice and Grand Marnier. Bake for 30 minutes or until the fruit is soft.

Remove the tray from the oven. Place the figs into 4 dessert bowls. Pour the juices into a pan over a moderate to high heat and let them bubble for a few minutes until they have reduced and are starting to thicken slightly. (Don’t overdo this, you don’t want them to caramelise.)

Spoon the sauce over the figs and serve with a dollop of double cream.
