Title: Confit tandoori chickpeas

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 1 hour 15 minutes

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Middle Eastern; vegetarian

Source: Ottolenghi Test Kitchen, p 105

Ingredients

  • Chilli:
    • 2 tins chickpeas
    • 10 cloves garlic, peeled
    • 30 g fresh ginger, peeled and finely cut
    • 400 g cherry tomatoes
    • 3 red chillies, mild
    • 1 tbsp tomato past
    • 2 tsp cumin seeds
    • 2 tsp coriander seeds
    • 1/2 tsp turmeric
    • 1/4 tsp chilli flakes (optional)
    • 2 tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
    • 1 tsp caster sugar
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 200 ml olive oil
  • Raita:
    • 200 g Greek-style yoghurt
    • 1/4 tsp salt
    • 1 clove garlic, peeled and crushed
    • 15 g mint leaves
    • 30 g coriander leaves
  • 2 limes
  • rice

Instructions

Peel the garlic, slit open the chillies, drain the chickpeas. Mix all ingredients together in a large saut√© pan for which you have a lid. Cover and transfer to the oven. Cook for 75 minutes.

Meanwhile blitz together yoghurt, coriander, mint, garlic and salt.

Cook a pot of rice suitable for four.

Serve chilli with rice, raita and lime wedges.
