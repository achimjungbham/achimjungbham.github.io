Title: Chilli con carne

Yield: 6 servings

Cooking time: 1 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 40 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: American

Source: Joy of Cooking

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • 2 large onions; chopped
  • 3 jalapeno peppers; or other fresh red chillies
  • 10 cloves garlic
  • 1 kg minced beef
  • 4 tbsp mild chilli powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 can chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tblsp vinegar
  • red wine (optional)
  • 1 pinch cinnamon
  • 2 can kidney beans

Instructions

Chop onions and peppers. Crush garlic. Saute in olive oil until soft.

Separately, fry the beef, possibly in several batches.

Mix everything together in a large sauce pan and add chilli powder, salt, canned tomatoes, and vinegar. Add some red wine or water.

Bring to a boil and leave to simmer for 90 minutes. Add more wine or water when necessary.

Add kidney beans and a hint of cinnamon and cook a tittle while longer.
