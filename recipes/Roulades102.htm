Title: Roulades

Yield: 4 portions

Category: Main

Cuisine: German

Source: Unser Kochbuch No 1, p. 138/139

Ingredients

  • 4 beef roulades; about 150g each
  • 2 tsp mustard
  • pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 4 slices speck
  • root vegetables
  • salt
  • 1 bay leaf
  • flour
  • 2 tbsp red wine
  • 125 ml sour cream

Instructions

Chop the onion and the parsley very finely. Mix with the mustard. Add plenty of pepper and some salt.

Place the strips of speck onto the roulades. (Alternatively, cut it into very small pieces and add to the filling.)

Divide the filling evenly between the four roulades and roll them up, securing them with cotton string.

Chop the root vegetables.

Heat the cooking oil and a large pan (for which you have a lid) and brown the roulades from all sides.

Add the root vegetables and fry a little longer. Add a cup of water, a bay leaf, and some salt. Cover with a lid and stew on low heat for 1 hour.

Remove the roulades and keep warm. Remove the bay leaf and blitz the cooking juices into a smooth pulp. Add the wine and sour cream, possibly mixed with a little flour for thickening. Check for seasoning. Optionally add a drop of Worcester sauce and some tomato pulp. Serve the sauce with the roulades.
