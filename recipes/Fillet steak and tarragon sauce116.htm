Title: Fillet steak and tarragon sauce

Yield: 2 servings

Cooking time: 5 minutes

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: French

Source: The French Kitchen; p. 146

Ingredients

  • 2 fillet steaks
  • 2 shallots; finely diced
  • 50 ml white wine
  • 30 ml double cream
  • 1 tsp grainy mustard
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tbsp tarragon; finely chopped
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

Chop the shallots and saut√© in a little olive oil. Add the wine and simmer for 3 minutes, then reduce the heat and add the cream, mustard, garlic, tarragon, and seasoning, and gently warm through. Do not allow to boil.

Fry the steaks as you like them (1.5 minutes each side for rare, 2.5 minutes for medium).
