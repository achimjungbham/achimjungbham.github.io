Title: Asparagus soup

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 40 minutes

Preparation time: 5 minutes

Category: Soup

Cuisine: German; vegetarian

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Unser Kochbuch No 1, p. 49

Ingredients

  • fresh asparagus:
    • 750 g asparagus spears
    • 1/2 l water
    • salt
  • soup:
    • 1 l chicken broth
    • 50 g butter
    • 4 tbsp flour
    • 2 egg yolks
    • 1/8 l light cream
    • 1/2 cup white wine
    • salt
    • white pepper

Instructions

Take about 4cm off the top of each asparagus spear and cook these tips for about 8 minutes in salt water. Drain and set aside.

Cut the remaining asparagus into small pieces and cook in the chicken broth for about 20 minutes. Using the hand blender, blitz to a fine soup.

Melt the butter in a separate pot, add the flour and cook for a short while. Add the soup in small portions and mix well. Cook on low heat for about 8 minutes.

Mix the egg yolks with the cream and add to the soup. Add the wine and season to taste. Finally, add the asparagus tips but don't cook any further.

Alternatively, use asparagus from a tin/glass and adjust the process accordingly.
