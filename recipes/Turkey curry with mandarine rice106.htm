Title: Turkey curry with mandarine rice

Yield: 4 portions

Category: Main

Cuisine: German

Source: Brigitte Di√§t, p. 95

Ingredients

  • cooking oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 onions
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 600 g turkey breast
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 300 ml chicken stock
  • lemon juice
  • salt
  • 2 tbsp hazelnuts
  • rice
  • 1 can mandarine wedges

Instructions

Put one cup of rice and two cups of water in a sauce pan with some salt and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to lowest setting.

Chop the onion, peel the garlic, cut the turkey breast into thin strips. Squeeze half a lemon.

Heat some oil in a large pan (for which you have a lid), sautee the onions and the crushed garlic until soft. Add curry powder and fry for 2 minutes. Add tomato puree, turkey strips, and broth. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes.

Season with lemon juice and salt, and perhaps a little sugar. Keep warm.

Roast hazelnuts in a dry pan, rub off their peels after they have cooled down, and chop coarsely. Mix with the mandarine wedges.

Serve rice, turkey, and hazelnut-mandarine mixture separately.
