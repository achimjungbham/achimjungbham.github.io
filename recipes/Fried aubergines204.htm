Title: Fried aubergines

Yield: 2 portions

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Category: Side

Cuisine: Tibetan

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Taste Tibet, p.97

Ingredients

  • 500 g aubergines
  • 4 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 large clove garlic
  • 2 1/2 cm piece of ginger
  • 1 mild chilli, red or green
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 flatbreads

Instructions

Quarter the aubergines lengthwise, then cut into thin (0.5cm) slices. Peel and finely slice the garlic and the ginger. De-seed the chilli and likewise slice finely.

Heat oil in a large saucepan for which you have a lid. Add all ingredients, including the salt. Fry for a little while, then cover for 5 minutes. Stir well and cook covered for 5 minutes more. Repeat. The aubergines should now have taken on a brown colour and be soft.

Serve with freshly toasted flatbread.

Works as a light meal for two or as a starter for four.
