Title: Potato gratin

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Side

Cuisine: German; vegetarian

Source: Chefkoch.de

Ingredients

  • 500 g salad potatoes
  • 1/8 litre milk
  • 1/8 litre single cream
  • 1 clove garlic
  • salt
  • pepper
  • rosemary
  • butter
  • 3 tbsp parmesan

Instructions

Peel potatoes and cut into thin slices. Peel garlic clove and finely cut fresh rosemary. Preheat oven to 220 deg.

In a saucepan, mix milk and cream, and add garlic, salt, pepper, and rosemary. Add the potato slices and cook gently for about 10 minutes.

In the meantime, grease a casserole dish with butter.

Pour the cream-potato mixture into the casserole dish and sprinkle the parmesan on top. Bake in hot oven for 15-20 minutes.
