Title: Beef and red wine stew

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 2 hours

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Italian

Source: Francesco Cosentino

Ingredients

  • 2 onions
  • olive oil
  • 1 kg beef
  • lots black pepper; freshly ground
  • salt
  • 1 bay leaf (optional)
  • 1 bottle red wine

Instructions

Saute onions in olive oil.

Cut beef into walnut sized pieces and brown separately from onions.

Add onions to beef and add plenty of freshly ground black pepper and a bay leaf. Add about half a tsp of salt.

Cover with red wine and bring to a boil. Simmer for 2 to 3 hours, adding more wine when necessary.

Add salt if necessary.
