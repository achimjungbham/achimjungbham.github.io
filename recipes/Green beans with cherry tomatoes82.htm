Title: Green beans with cherry tomatoes

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Complete meal

Cuisine: American; vegetarian

Source: Three Rivers Cookbook, p.115

Ingredients

  • 750 g fresh green beans
  • 100 g butter
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tblsp sugar
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp basil
  • 1 pinch pepper
  • 300 g cherry tomatoes
  • 800 g potatoes

Instructions

Peal potatoes and cut into smaller pieces. Wash and de-string beans.

Cook potatoes in 1 cup of salt water for about 20 minutes. Cook beans in 1 cup of water for about the same time. In a separate pot, melt butter. Add crushed garlic, sugar, salt, basil and pepper. Swish cherry tomatoes around until barely soft and heated but not squishy. Add beans and mix well.

For potatoes, put them in a serving dish, add some butter and some salt.
