Title: Spaghetti Carbonara

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Italian

Source: Pasta cookbook; p. 114

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion
  • 8 strips strips pancetta
  • 350 g spaghetti
  • 4 eggs
  • 4 tbsp creme fraiche
  • 4 tbsp parmesan; grated
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook to taste.
Finely chop onion and saute in olive oil in a large sauce pan for about 5 minutes.
Add the pancetta strips and saute for 10 minutes more.
Put eggs, creme fraiche, and grated parmesan into a bowl. Add plenty of pepper, then beat everything together well.
Drain the pasta and tip into the pan with the onion and pancetta. Toss well. Turn off heat and add egg mixture. Toss vigorously so that it cooks lightly and coats the pasta.
Taste for seasoning.
Divide among four bowls and sprinkle with pepper and extra parmesan.
