Title: Semmelknödel (bread dumplings)

Yield: 6 servings

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Preparation time: 40 minutes

Category: Side

Cuisine: German

Source: Graefe Unzer: Unser Kochbuch, p.315

Ingredients

  • 10 rolls ;slightly stale, about 500g
  • 250 ml milk
  • 3 eggs
  • salt
  • nutmeg
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bunch parsley
  • 2 tblsp butter

Instructions

Cut rolls into thin slices and put into mixing bowl. Add milk and check for consistency.
Add eggs, salt, and nutmeg. Mix well.

Melt butter in a pan and meanwhile finely chop onion and parsley.
Add onion and parsley to pan and gently cook until the onion is soft.
Add this mixture to the rolls and mix well.
Let stand for about 20 minutes.

Take a large pot and bring plenty of salt water to a boil.
Using wet hands, form dumplings, and put them into the water. Leave to simmer for 15 to 20 minutes.

Drain well before serving.

Notes

Excellent side dish for meaty dishes such as roulade or pot roast.
