Title: Pancakes with Chinese Vegetables

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Chinese

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Ingredients

  • Pancakes:
    • 4 eggs
    • 500 ml milk
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 350 g flour
    • oil
  • Filling:
    • 2 tbsp oil
    • 2 leek
    • 1 red pepper
    • 2 tsp ginger (optional)
    • 1 red finger chilli (optional)
    • 400 g fresh mushrooms (or canned ones)
    • 400 g fresh bean sprouts
    • 6 tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 tsp rice vinegar
    • salt (optional)
    • pepper

Instructions

Mix eggs with half the milk and add enough flour to make a stiff dough. Beat with electric mixer until very smooth. Now add more milk until the mixture become quite runny again.

Heat a pan with oil and bake your pancakes as thinly as you can. Put them in the oven at 70 deg to keep warm.

Meanwhile cut leeks and pepper very thinly and cook in oil a large pot. Slice the mushrooms and add to the pot. Add very finely julienned ginger (if using). Stir fry until the peppers are reasonably soft, then add sprouts, cover, and cook for about 5 min more.

Season to taste with soy sauce, a little salt and plenty of pepper.

To eat, add about 2 tbsp of vegetable filling to a pancake, roll up, and eat with knife and fork.
