Title: Cauliflower soup

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Category: Soup

Cuisine: German

Rating: 4.0/5 stars

Source: Brigitte Di√§t, p. 120

Ingredients

  • 1 head cauliflower
  • 800 ml vegetable or chicken broth
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 250 ml light cream
  • salt
  • pepper
  • nutmeg
  • lemon juice
  • 250 g cooked ham
  • parsley
  • crusty bread

Instructions

Break up the cauliflower head into individual florets, and wash them. Put them in the pressure cooker with the broth, close the lid and cook at high pressure for 2 minutes. In the meantime, cut the ham into pieces, mix the egg yolks with the cream, and chop a handful of parsley leaves.

Once you are able to open the pressure cooker, take out about half the florets and set aside. Blitz the remaining contents with the hand blender and add the cream-egg mixture. Season to taste and add a little bit of lemon juice. Once you are happy with the result, return the reserved florets, the ham, and the parsley.

Serve with crusty bread.
