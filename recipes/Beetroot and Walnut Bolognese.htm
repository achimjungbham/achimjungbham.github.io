Title: Beetroot and Walnut Bolognese

Serves: 2

Preparation time: 30 minutes

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Vegan

Source: Meera Sodha (The Guardian), Jan 2025

Ingredients

  • Sauce
    • 4 tbsp cooking oil
    • 4 cloves garlic
    • 2 cm ginger
    • 250 g (chestnut) mushrooms
    • 250 g beetroots (pre-cooked)
  • Spices
    • 1 tbsp sundried tomato paste
    • 1 tbsp toban djan (or another sweet and hot chilli sauce)
  • Further sauce ingredients
    • 40 g walnuts
    • 100 g spring onions
  • Pasta
    • 180 g spaghetti
  • Side
    • salad

Instructions

Peel the garlic and ginger and cut very finely. Clean the mushrooms and cut into small cubes. Heat the oil in a large sauce pan and gently fry garlic and ginger, then add the mushrooms and fry until soft. Add the beetroot cubes to allow them to heat up.

Dump everything into a blender, add chilli sauce and tomato paste and blend to a smooth consistency, adding some warm water if the mixture is too dry. Check for seasoning, then return to the pan.

Add finely chopped walnuts and thinly sliced spring onions and keep warm.

Cook the spaghetti to your preferred consistency and add to the pan. Mix well and serve immediately with the salad.
