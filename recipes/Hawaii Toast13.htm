Title: Hawaii Toast

Yield: 12 toasts

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: German

Ingredients

  • 12 slices bread
  • butter
  • 12 slices cooked smoked ham
  • 12 slices pineapple
  • 12 slices cheese; edam or gouda

Instructions

Pre-heat oven to 160 deg C, or use grill.

Pre-toast the bread, leave to cool a bit.

Butter the toasts, add a slice of ham, then pineapple, then cheese. Put on a baking tray and leave in oven/grill for about 10 minutes (until the cheese begins to bubble and the bubbles begin to brown).
