Title: Lemon cream sauce with pasta

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Complete meal

Cuisine: German; vegetarian

Source: Gabi

Ingredients

  • 300 g sugar snaps
  • 1 lemon
  • 5 spring onions
  • 2 clove garlic
  • 1 box cress
  • 100 g Gorgonzola
  • 2 tblsp butter
  • 200 g peas; frozen
  • 100 ml vegetable stock
  • 250 g light cream
  • salt
  • pepper
  • nutmeg
  • 400 g spaghetti

Instructions

Heat salted water for pasta.

Wash sugar snaps, cut off the ends and chop into bite-sized pieces. Boil in salted water for 4 minutes. Drain and leave.

Wash the lemon, grate off the zest, and squeeze out all juice. Wash spring onions and cut into thin slices. Peel garlic. Cut off the cress and chop coarsely. Crumble the cheese.

Melt butter in a small sauce pan. Gently fry peas, spring onions, and garlic. Add the vegetable stock and cleave to cook for 5 minutes.

Add cream, cheese, and lemon zest. Heat until the cheese has melted. Season with salt, pepper and nutmeg. Add lemon juice to taste (about 1 tsp). Mix in the sugar snaps.

Serve cress separately.
