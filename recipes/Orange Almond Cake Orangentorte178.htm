Title: Orange Almond Cake (Orangentorte)

Category: Cake

Cuisine: German

Ingredients

  • For a 26cm non-stick spring form cake tin:
  • 7 eggs
  • 240 g sugar
  • 2 large oranges
  • 30 g flour
  • 80 g sponge cake crumbs (small flan or ladyfingers)
  • 280 g ground (unpeeled) almonds
  • 100 g marmelade (orange jelly)
  • 100 g flaked almonds
  • 7 candied orange slices (optional)
  • butter/margarine (for greasing the tin)
  • bread crumbs

Instructions

Pre-heat oven to 200 deg Celsius.

Butter tin and scatter bread crumbs. Separate eggs. Zest and juice oranges.

Whisk egg yolks with half the sugar until frothy, then add zest and juice of the oranges.

Beat the egg whites with the other half of the sugar and fold into egg yolk mixture.

Sift the flour on top and add biscuit crumbs and ground almonds. Carefully fold everything together.

Pour mixture into the tin and smoothen the top. Bake for 30-40 minutes.

Allow the cake to cool somewhat on a wire rack, then spread heated orange jelly on top.

Roast the flaked almonds in a bit of melted butter until slightly brown. Scatter them onto the top of the cake. If you like, decorate with candied orange slices.

Enjoy with good friends!
