Title: Chilli Senza Carne

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Mexican; vegetarian

Rating: 4.0/5 stars

Source: Robbie

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 4 mushrooms, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, diced or crushed
  • tomato puree
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • chilli flakes
  • 600 g veggie mince
  • 300 ml veggie stock
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 50 g dark chocolate (optional)
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained

Instructions

Start a pot of rice.

Dice onion and mushrooms. Peel garlic. Drain beans. Open chopped tomato can.

Heat pan with oil. Fry onions on low/medium heat until soft, then add mushrooms. Fry until they start to get soft too, then add garlic, tomato puree and spices.

Whilst the onions & co are cooking, grate some pepper over the veggie Mince and fry in a small amount of oil to crisp a little. Once done, add to the onions & co.

Add stock, chopped tomatoes, a good bit of salt and pepper, and grate the dark chocolate over the pan. Allow to cook for 5-10mins.

Add kidney beans. Stir to combine, add salt+pepper to taste, and cook for 5-10mins until the beans are good to eat.

Make sure to cook until the dish is reduced to the desired texture - quite moist, but but solid enough.
