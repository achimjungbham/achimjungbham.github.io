Title: Lentil soup

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Preparation time: 5 minutes

Category: Soup

Cuisine: German; vegetarian

Source: Wolfram Siebeck; p54

Ingredients

  • 250 g lentils; eg Puy
  • 1 l chicken stock
  • 1/4 l light cream
  • cayenne pepper
  • salt
  • 4 frankfurters (optional)

Instructions

Wash lentils in a strainer. Cook in water for 10 minutes. Drain and transfer to chicken broth and cook until soft.

Blend the soup with a hand blender. Add cream. Season with salt and cayenne pepper to taste. Add a little vinegar. Add frankfurters to warm up.
