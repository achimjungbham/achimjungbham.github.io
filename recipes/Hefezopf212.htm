Title: Hefezopf

Yield: 1 Cake

Cooking time: 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 2 hours

Category: Baking

Cuisine: German

Source: James

Ingredients

  • Yeast dough:
    • 250 ml single cream
    • 500 g general purpose flour
    • 1 sachet dried yeast
    • 80 g sugar
    • 1 sachet vanilla sugar
    • zest from one lemon
    • 1 pinch salt
    • 2 medium eggs
    • 1 egg white
  • Glazing:
    • 1 egg yolk
    • 1 tbsp milk

Instructions

Heat the cream to lukewarm. Mix flour with dried yeast and other ingredients. Mix together and knead well.

Allow to rise to double the size.

Cut into four pieces and form each into a strand of about 40 cm length. Weave into a "Zopf".

Line a baking sheet with paper or grease it well with butter. Brush the top of the Zopf with the milk-egg yolk mixture, then
place it on the baking sheet and allow it to rise for another 20 minutes.

Bake at 180 deg (or 160 if using a fan-assisted oven) for about 30 minutes.
