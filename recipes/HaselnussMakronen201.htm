Title: Haselnuss-Makronen

Yield: 80 Pieces

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Preparation time: 40 minutes

Category: Christmas cookies

Cuisine: German

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Chefkoch.de

Original Page from www.chefkoch.de

Ingredients

  • 4 egg whites
  • 250 g icing sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 lemon, zested
  • 250 g hazelnuts, ground
  • 80 hazelnuts, whole

Instructions

Beat the egg whites until stiff, add the icing sugar and mix well into a shiny mass. Add ground hazelnuts, cinnamon, and the lemon zest. Mix gently.

Line two baking sheets with non-stick paper and place walnut-sized lumps of dough on it (leaving about 2cm of space between each of them). Put a whole hazelnut into the center of each.

Leave to dry for three hours, then bake at 120 C for 20 minutes. It may be necessary to bake for another 3-5 minutes with bottom heat only.

Leave to cool and store in a tightly closing metal box.
