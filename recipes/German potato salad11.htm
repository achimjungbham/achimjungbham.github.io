Title: German potato salad

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 5 minutes

Preparation time: 40 minutes

Category: Side

Cuisine: German; vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 800 g new potatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 onion
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • oil
  • vinegar
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

Parboil potatoes. Leave to cool and peel.

In a large bowl, mix vegetable stock with sunflower oil and a little vinegar. Add finely chopped onion and season to taste. Mix well.

Slice into the bowl the potatoes and the cucumber. Mix regularly and check for taste. Add oil, vinegar, stock, salt and pepper as necessary.

Ideally, serve warm.
