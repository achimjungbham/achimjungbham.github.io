Title: Gnocchi with Gorgonzola sauce

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Preparation time: 5 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Italian; vegetarian

Source: Pasta cookbook; p. 128

Ingredients

  • 600 g fresh gnocchi
  • 150 g Gorgonzola cheese
  • 150 ml cream
  • 1 pinch sugar
  • 2 tsp sage
  • salt; very little
  • black pepper
  • 1 head salad

Instructions

Start a pot with salted water for the gnocchi

Cut away any rind from the cheese, cut into small pieces. Wash and chop finely the sage leaves.

Gently heat cream and gorgonzola in a medium saucepan. Add sugar and plenty of black pepper. Stir frequently. Remove from heat when cheese has melted and add sage.

Cook gnocchi according to instructions. Drain well and add to gorgonzola sauce. Mix and leave to soak for 5 minutes. Then serve without delay.
