Title: Hackepeters Gemüseeintopf (vegetable stew)

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 50 minutes

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Main

Cuisine: German

Rating: 4.0/5 stars

Source: Unser Kochbuch No 1, p. 327

Ingredients

  • 100 g lardons
  • 2 onions
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 400 g minced pork, or veggie mince
  • 500 g green beans
  • 4 potatoes
  • 1/2 litre broth
  • 2 red peppers
  • 1/2 can chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp sage
  • salt

Instructions

Peel the potatoes and cut into small cubes. Clean the beans and cut into short sections.

Dice the onions and peel the garlic. Heat some cooking oil in a large saucepan. Sauté onions, garlic, and lardons.

Add minced pork (or veggie mince) and let it get some colour, stirring all the time.

Add broth, potatoes, and beans. Cook for 30 minutes (4 minutes in the pressure cooker).

In the meantime, clean the peppers and cut into bite-sized chunks. Open the can of tomatoes.

After the first cooking time is over, add peppers, tomatoes, paprika and sage, and cook for a further 20 minutes (3 minutes in the pressure cooker. Be careful, though, and heat it up gently, as it may stick to the bottom of the pot.)

Check for seasoning and serve.
