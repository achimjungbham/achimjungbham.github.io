Title: Leek Quiche (Lauchtorte)

Yield: 6 portions

Category: Starter

Cuisine: French

Source: Siebeck p. 129

Ingredients

  • 1 kg leeks
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 250 ml cream
  • 3 tbsp creme fraiche
  • 150 g pancetta
  • white wine
  • butter
  • pepper
  • salt
  • Pie shell:
    • 200 g flour
    • 75 g butter
    • 1 egg yolk
    • salt

Instructions

Prepare the dough for the pie shell in the usual way: Mix the dry ingredients well and add egg yolk with enough cold water to make a soft dough. Leave to rest in the fridge.

Pre-heat oven to 200 deg Celsius.

Cut the speck or pancetta into very small pieces.

Cut the washed and dried leek stalks into 3cm long pieces and slice these thinly lengthwise. Sautee in butter but don't overcook. Season well and add the wine. Cover and let stew for 10 minutes. Drain and let cool.

Mix eggs, egg yolk, creme fraiche and season well.

Line a large baking tin with the thinly rolled dough. Place first the speck, then the leek pieces on top. Pour in the egg-cream mixture.

Place on the bottom (!) of the hot oven but reduce heat immediately to 160 deg. Baking time 40-50 minutes.
