Title: Wild Rice with Currants

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: English; vegetarian

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Ottolenghi

Ingredients

  • Rice:
    • 1/2 cup wild rice
    • 2 cups basmati rice
  • Chickpeas:
    • 2 tsp cumin seeds
    • olive oil
    • 1 1/2 tsp curry powder
    • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • Fried onions:
    • 1 medium onion
    • sunflower oil
    • flour
  • Other ingredients:
    • 100 g currants
    • 2 tbsp parsley
    • 1 tbsp cilantro
    • 1 tbsp dill
    • seasoning

Instructions

Cook the basmati rice using your preferred method. Cook the wild rice separately in plenty salted water for about 40 minutes.

Add some olive oil to a saucepan over high heat. Add cumin seeds and curry powder and cook very briefly, then add the chickpeas and 1/4 tsp salt. Stir for about a minute or two then transfer to a large bowl.

Finely slice the onions and turn them in plenty of flour. Using a suitable pan, heat a good amount of sunflower oil and fry the onion in batches until nicely browned, crisp, but not burned. Take them out on let them drain on kitchen paper. Sprinkle salt on top.

Add the two kinds of rice, the currants and the herbs to the chickpeas. Add plenty of pepper and mix well. Check for salt and mix some more.

Serve warm or at room temperature. Serve the onions on top of the rice or separately.
