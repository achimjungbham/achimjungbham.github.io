Title: Feijoada (vegetarian)

Yield: 6 portions

Cooking time: 5 hours

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Brazilian

Source: Ottolenghi (Guardian)

Ingredients

  • 500 g black beans
  • olive oil
  • 15 g ginger
  • 2 green chillies
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 2 onions
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tsp salt
  • black pepper

Instructions

Soak the beans for at least 24 hours in cold water.

Chop the onions, chillies, garlic, and ginger.

Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a large pot and fry the onions, chillies, garlic, and ginger for about 5 minutes.

Add beans and spices and about 1.2 litres of water. Bring to the boil and cook on low heat for at least an hour and a half (30 minutes in the pressure cooker). Remove the lid and cook for 30 minutes more. Meanwhile, cook a pot of rice.
