Title: Mousse au chocolat

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 1/2 days

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Dessert

Cuisine: French

Source: Pittsburgh Nationality Rooms Recipe Book, p.204

Ingredients

  • 100 g dark chocolate
  • 2 eggs; separated
  • 2 egg whites
  • 2 tblsp kirsch
  • 2 tblsp butter
  • 1 tblsp sugar

Instructions

Melt chocolate over hot water in a double boiler. Remove and add sugar, kirsch, and egg yolks, stirring vigorously. Add butter a bit at a time and stir to blend. Allow mixture to cool.

In the meantime, beat egg whites until stiff. Add some of the egg white to the chocolate mixture to make it creamier.

Put egg whites in a china or glass bowl and carefully blend chocolate into egg whites with a fork.

Put in fridge overnight.
