Title: Leek and raisins (Rosinenlauch)

Yield: 4 portions

Category: Main

Cuisine: German; vegetarian

Source: Kochvergnuegen p. 195

Ingredients

  • 500 g leeks
  • 3 tbsp raisins
  • 40 ml Sherry (Fino)
  • 2 eggs
  • 200 ml single cream
  • nutmeg
  • pepper
  • 80 g Cashews
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • butter
  • 400 g tagliatelle

Instructions

Soak the raisins in the sherry.

Preheat oven to 200 deg Celsius.

Wash the leeks and cut into 10 cm long pieces. Half them lengthwise. Boil in salt water for 3 minutes. Rinse in cold water.

Mix cream with beaten eggs, nutmeg, salt, and pepper.

Place the leek sticks into a casserole dish and pour the egg-cream mixture over them. Put in hot oven and bake for 20 minutes.

In the meantime, chop the cashew nuts coarsely and roast in a dry pan until they show colour. Add butter and curry powder and heat a little longer. Add raisins and sherry and bring to a rapid boil until most of the liquid is gone.

Remove casserole from oven, spread the mixture evenly across the leeks, add some small pieces of butter, and bake for another 5 minutes.

Serve with tagliatelle.
