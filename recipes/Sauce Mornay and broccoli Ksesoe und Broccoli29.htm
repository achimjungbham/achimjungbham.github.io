Title: Sauce Mornay and broccoli (Käsesoße und Broccoli)

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: German; vegetarian

Source: Trifonopoulo and Koderisch

Ingredients

  • 2 tblsp butter
  • 2 tblsp flour
  • milk; and/or light cream
  • 180 g Gruyere
  • salt
  • pepper
  • nutmeg
  • 600 g broccoli
  • 400 g pasta; e.g. Penne Rigate

Instructions

Grate the Gruyere. Wash the broccoli and cut into medium pieces. Chuck out the stems.

Put water into a pasta pot, salt, and put on oven to heat.

Put broccoli into a separate pot with water. Sprinkle with salt and cover. Cook for about 10 minutes.

Meanwhile, melt butter in a small saucepan, add flour and fry gently. Turn up heat, add milk (or light cream) a bit at a time, stirring vigorously, until you have a fairly runny sauce bechamel. Reduce heat and season with salt, pepper and nutmeg. Add the cheese, and stir to dissolve. Cover and leave over very low heat.
