Title: Sweet potato soup

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Soup

Cuisine: American; vegetarian

Source: Waitrose magazine

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 tsp coriander seeds; crushed
  • 2 red chillies; deseeded and finely chopped
  • 1 butternut squash
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes
  • 1 tin tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1 1/2 l vegetable stock

Instructions

Heat oil in a large pot. Chop onion and saute until soft. Add coriander seeds and chilli and cook for another 2 minutes.

Peel sweet potato and butternut squash and cut into large chunks. Add to onions and fry for about 5 minutes.

Add chopped tomatoes and vegetable stock. Bring to a boil and simmer gently for 15 minutes, or until the vegetables are soft.

Blend using the hand blender. Season to taste.
