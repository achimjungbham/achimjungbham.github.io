Title: Portobello mushrooms

Yield: 4 port

Category: Starter

Cuisine: English; vegetarian

Source: Vegetarian; p. 240

Ingredients

  • 8 portobello mushrooms
  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/4 cup rolled oats
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp Tabasco sauce
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cooking oil

Instructions

Pre-heat oven to 190 deg Celsius.

Lightly grease a baking sheet.

Remove mushroom stalks and chop them coarsely. Chop the onion.

Heat the oil in a small saucepan and sautee the onion, crushed garlic, and mushroom stalks until softened. Stir in the oats and cook for 1 minute more.

Stir in the tomatoes and hot pepper sauce. Season to taste.

Arrange the mushroom caps on the baking sheet, gills up. Divide the stuffing among them. Sprinkle the pine nuts and Parmesan cheese over them.

Bake for 25 minutes until the mushrooms are tender and the topping is golden brown.
