Title: Pavlova

Yield: 1 cake

Cooking time: 1 hour

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Category: Baking

Cuisine: English

Source: Tanis and James

Ingredients

  • Meringue:
    • 4 egg whites
    • 250 g sugar
    • 1 tsp white vinegar
    • 1 tsp corn flour
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract or 1 sachet vanilla sugar
  • Topping:
    • 3 tbsp icing sugar
    • 350 ml whipping cream
    • fresh fruit

Instructions

Whisk egg whites to stiff peaks then add sugar 1 tbsp at a time. Keep whisking until done and glossy.

Whisk in cornflour, vinegar and vanilla.

Line a baking sheet with parchment and spread the egg whites in a circular disk shape.

Bake for 1 hour at 150 deg Celsius. Let cool in the oven.

Put the meringue on a serving platter and add the topping of whipped cream and fruit.
