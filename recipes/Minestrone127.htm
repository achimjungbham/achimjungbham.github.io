Title: Minestrone

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 45 minutes

Preparation time: 25 minutes

Category: Soup

Cuisine: Italian; vegetarian

Source: Guardian

Ingredients

  • 1 onion; chopped
  • 2 carrots; chopped (optional)
  • 2 sticks sticks celery; chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic; crushed
  • 500 g Savoy cabbage or Swiss chard; roughly shredded
  • 1 tin chopped tomatos
  • 2 tins cannelli beans
  • 1 sprig thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cube vegetable stock
  • black pepper
  • nutmeg
  • 1 drop Worcester sauce
  • parmesan; to serve
  • olive oil; to serve

Instructions

Prepare the fresh vegetables.

Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a large pan and gently saut√© onions, carrots, celery, and garlic for 15 minutes.

Meanwhile, bring a pot of salt water to a boil and blanche the cabbage until tender.

Add tinned tomatoes, bay leave, thyme, and beans (drained and rinsed, if you wish) to vegetables and add 1.5 l of boiling water. Add stock cube, black pepper and salt (if necessary). Simmer for 30 minutes.

Remove bay leaf and thyme. Add a drop of Worcester sauce and some freshly grated nutmeg. Check seasoning.

Add blanched cabbage to soup and cook a little further until everything has a pleasant consistency.

Serve with grated parmesan and slug of olive oil added to each plate.
