Title: Spaghetti Bolognese

Yield: 6 portions

Cooking time: 1 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Main

Cuisine: Italian

Source: BBC Good Food

Original Page from www.bbcgoodfood.com

Ingredients

  • Bacon:
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • 100 g streaky bacon or pancetta
  • Vegetables:
    • 2 onions
    • 2 carrots
    • 2 celery sticks
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1 red chilli (optional)
  • Meat:
    • 500 g mince; beef or pork
  • Sauce:
    • 2 cans chopped tomatoes
    • 125 ml red wine
    • 2 tbsp tomato puree
    • 1 cube beef stock
    • 2 fresh bay leaves
    • 2 sprigs rosemary
    • 1 tsp oregano
    • 1 bunch basil
    • freshly ground black pepper
    • 6 cherry tomatoes
  • 600 g spaghetti
  • 75 g parmesan; grated
  • seasoning

Instructions

Put a large saucepan on a medium heat and add 1 tbsp olive oil. Add the bacon and fry for 10 mins until golden and crisp.

Reduce the heat and add the onion, carrot, celery, garlic, and chilli (if using) then fry for 10 mins. Stir the veg often until they soften.

Remove from pan, add more oil to the pan if necessary and brown the minced meat over high heat.

Add the vegetables, tinned tomatoes, chopped basil, oregano, bay leaves, tomato pur√©e, stock cube, wine, ground black pepper, and cherry tomatoes. Bring to a boil, cover, and reduce heat to a simmer.

Cook for 1 1/4 hrs, stirring occasionally, until you have a rich, thick sauce.

Cook the spaghetti.

Before serving, add the Parmesan to the Bolognese, check the seasoning and stir.

Serve with grated Parmesan and extra basil leaves.
