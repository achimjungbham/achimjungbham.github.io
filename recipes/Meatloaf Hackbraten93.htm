Title: Meatloaf (Hackbraten)

Yield: 4 portions

Category: Main

Cuisine: German

Source: Kochvergnuegen p. 127

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 500 g minced pork
  • 1 egg
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • pepper
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • cooking oil
  • 125 ml broth

Instructions

Soak the bread in water. Then squeeze out the water with your hands and place in a mixing bowl. Add minced meat, egg, crushed garlic, salt, pepper, and oregano. Mix well.

Heat oil in a large pot. Form a nice loaf from the meat mixture and brown it in the hot oil from all sides. Be careful that it doesn't break as you turn it.

Reduce heat and add broth. Cover and let stew for 40 minutes.

Remove meatloaf from pot and set aside. Add a milk flour mixture to the cooking juices and bring to a boil. Season with some tomato paste, Worcester sauce, and more salt and pepper if necessary.

Cut the meatloaf into slices and serve the gravy separately.
