Title: Pork goulash

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Meat

Cuisine: German

Source: Luise Harrer; p. 57

Ingredients

  • 500 g pork
  • oil
  • 1 tblsp flour
  • 1 tblsp tomato puree
  • 1 large onion
  • salt
  • paprika
  • pepper
  • 1/2 bay leaf
  • 200 ml meat stock
  • red wine (optional)

Instructions

Cut meat into bite-sized pieces. Chop the onion. Mix both with the flour.

Heat oil in large pot (or pressure cooker) until it starts to smoke. Fry meat-onion-flour mixture without stirring. Turn over and leave to fry. Same once more.

Add stock, pepper, paprika, bay leaf, cover and simmer until done. In the pressure cooker this takes 12 minutes.

Check for seasoning before serving.
