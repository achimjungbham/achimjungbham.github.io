Title: Moroccan Tagine

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Preparation time: 25 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Middle Eastern; vegetarian

Source: Quorn website

Ingredients

  • 350 g Quorn chicken style pieces
  • olive oil
  • 1 onion; preferably red
  • 2 peppers; yellow and red
  • 2 courgettes
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tblsp harissa paste
  • 75 g dried apricots; chopped
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 200 ml vegetable stock
  • 1 tblsp honey
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • 1 bunch fresh coriander (optional)
  • 200 g cous cous
  • 450 ml vegetable stock
  • 2 tblsp butter

Instructions

Cut peppers into small cubes or slices. Chop the onion. Cut the courgettes into cubes about 2cm width. Prepare couscous with boiling hot salt water or vegetable stock.

Fry quorn pieces in oil until nicely browned. Take out, cover and set aside.
Add more oil and stir fry onion and peppers. Return the quorn pieces to the pan.
Add courgette, crushed garlic cloves, harissa paste, cumin, apricots, tinned tomatoes, and vegetable stock. Simmer for about 10 minutes. Finally add honey and check for seasoning.

Melt a dollop of butter on the warm couscous, and loosen up with a fork.
