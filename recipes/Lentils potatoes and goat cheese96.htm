Title: Lentils, potatoes and goat cheese

Yield: 4 portions

Category: Main

Cuisine: German; vegetarian

Source: Siebeck p. 22

Ingredients

  • 200 g Puy lentils
  • 1 stock cube
  • 800 g baby potatoes
  • 4 shallots
  • 200 g goat cheese
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 2 tsp dried tarragon

Instructions

Cook the lentils in boiling salt water for 10 minutes. Discard cooking water and rinse lentils well. Return to pot and cook in chicken broth for another 15 minutes.

Boil the potatoes.

Cut the shallots as finely as you can manage. Peel the garlic cloves.

Mix olive oil, vinegar, shallots, crushed garlic, tarragon, salt, and pepper.

Cut the cheese into cubes.

Drain the lentils and mix with the vinaigrette and the cheese.

Arrange in the middle of four plates, surrounding the lentil mixture with the potatoes cut into halves.
