Title: Zucchini Soup

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Soup

Cuisine: English

Source: Vegetarian; p. 174

Ingredients

  • 2 tblsp olive oil
  • 1 tblsp butter
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 kg zucchini; (i.e., courgettes)
  • 450 ml vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • pepper
  • 100 g blue cheese; e.g. St Augur
  • 250 ml single cream
  • 1 French bread

Instructions

Chop onions. Heat oil and butter in a large saucepan (or the pressure cooker). Saute onions until soft.

In the meantime, wash and partially peel zucchini. Cut into a pieces and add to onions. Add oregano and pepper. Cook over medium heat for about 10 minutes, stirring frequently.

Pour in stock, bring to a boil, and allow to simmer for about 30 minutes (5 minutes in the pressure cooker).

Stir in the cheese and when melted use a hand blender to process into a smooth soup. Add cream and check for seasoning.

Serve with fresh French bread.
