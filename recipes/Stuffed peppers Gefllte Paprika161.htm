Title: Stuffed peppers (Gefüllte Paprika)

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 5 minutes

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Main

Cuisine: German

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Brigitte Diät, p. 100

Ingredients

  • 4 large green peppers (or 6 small ones)
  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 medium onions
  • parsley
  • 200 g cooked ham
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 150 ml single cream

Instructions

Cook the rice and leave to cool.

Cut off the top of the peppers and remove everything from inside. Chop the onions finely, and put half of them into a pot with cooking oil, the other half in a mixing bowl. Add cooked rice, chopped parsley, and diced ham to the mixing bowl. Mix well (without salt and pepper!).

Saute the other half of the onions in the cooking oil until soft. Add paprika and saute some more, then add tomato paste and cook some more. Then add the broth and season to taste.

Meanwhile stuff the peppers and put them upright into the pot. Add a lid and cook for about 10 minutes (until the peppers are al-dente). In the pressure cooker, this only takes a minute or two at full pressure.

After cooking, add the cream to the broth.
