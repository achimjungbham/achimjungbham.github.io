Title: Courgette salad

Yield: 4 portions

Category: Starter

Cuisine: Italian; vegetarian

Source: Die echte italienische KÃ¼che, p. 264

Ingredients

  • 500 g small courgettes
  • fresh mint
  • basil
  • flat-leaf parsley
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 100 ml white wine vinegar
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

Cut the courgettes into 1/2 cm thick slices. In a large sauce pan heat plenty of olive oil. Fry the courgette slices until they are light brown, possibly in batches. Drain them on kitchen roll paper.

Cut the mint, parsley, and basil leaves into fine strips. Mix the vinegar with half the herbs and all the (peeled and crushed) garlic.

Arrange the courgette slices on a large platter (or four individual plates) and season with salt and black pepper. Drizzle the vinegar-herb mixture onto them and then nicely garnish with the remaining herbs. Put in the fridge and marinate for 3 to 4 hours.
