Title: Tortilla chicken

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Main

Cuisine: Mexican

Source: Days in Valencia Ave

Ingredients

  • 2 red peppers
  • 2 onions; thinly sliced
  • olive oil
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 tblsp mild chilli powder
  • juice of 1 lime
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 8 clove garlic
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 bunch cilantro; (coriander leaves)
  • 6 tblsp sour cream

Instructions

Slice peppers and onions. Gently fry peppers for 4 minutes in olive oil, then add onions and fry for another 10 minutes or until tender.

In the meantime, cut chicken breast into bite-sized chunks, peel garlic cloves, and chop coriander leaves.

When the peppers and onions are done, take them out and place them into large bowl, cover and keep warm. Fry the chicken pieces in more olive oil until slightly browned.

Add chilli powder, lime juice, crushed garlic, 1.5 tblsp olive oil, and salt, and mix well. Add the pepper-onion mixture, cover and keep over low heat.

In the meantime, 4 stack tortillas with wet kitchen paper towels in a bowl, and microwave for a one minute. Fill each with the chicken-pepper-onion mixture, spread some sour cream on top and sprinkle with coriander leaves.

Serve immediately, while preparing the second batch.
