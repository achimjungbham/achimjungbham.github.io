Title: Zucchini-Pepper Preserve

Yield: 5 kg

Cooking time: 5 minutes

Preparation time: 1 hour

Category: Preserves

Cuisine: German

Source: Sigrid

Ingredients

  • 2 kg zucchini
  • 500 g red and yellow peppers
  • 500 g onions
  • 1 l Gurkenaufguss; or light spicy vinegar
  • 1/2 l apple juice
  • 100 g sugar
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1-2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp Gurkengewuerz; or spices for cornichons/chutney

Instructions

Prepare the vegetables.

Bring all other ingredients to a strong boil. Add vegetable pieces and cook for 5 minutes, making sure they don't become mushy.

Fill several glass containers with the hot mixture and seal tightly.
