Title: Apple spice muffins

Yield: 12 rolls

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Category: Breads

Cuisine: American

Rating: 4.0/5 stars

Source: Three Rivers Cookbook, p.38

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 cup finely chopped apples
  • 1/4 cup raisins

Instructions

Mix dry ingredients together and add apples and raisins.

Mix milk with egg, stir in melted butter. Pour over dry ingredients and stir just until moistened; will be lumpy.

Fill well-greased muffin tins 2/3 full. Bake at 200 deg for 20 to 25 minutes.
