Title: Cheese choux ring with mushroom filling

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 25 minutes

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: French; vegetarian

Source: Heston Blumental

Ingredients

  • Pastry:
    • 85 g butter
    • 200 ml water
    • 170 g plain flour
    • 5 eggs, beaten
    • 185 g mature cheddar
    • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
    • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 30 g parmesan
  • Filling:
    • 600 g mushrooms, sliced
    • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
    • 2 tbsp parsely, chopped
    • 80 g butter
    • 3 tbsp olive oil
    • 50 g pinenuts
    • seasoning

Instructions

Prehead oven to 175 C.
Boil water and pour into mixing bowl. Add butter and allow it to melt. Add flour and mix with an electric whisk. Keep whisking as you slowly add the eggs. Add cheese, mustard, cayenne pepper, and salt. Arrange on a baking tray in the shape of a ring. Sprinkle with parmesan. Put in oven and bake for 25 minutes.
Prepare mushrooms, garlic, and parsley. Heat butter and olive oil in a pan and saut√© ingredients for about 5 minutes. Add pine nuts or toast them separately. Season liberally. Aim to have the mushrooms ready when the choux ring comes out of the oven.
Eat without delay.
