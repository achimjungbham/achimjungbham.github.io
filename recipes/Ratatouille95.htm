Title: Ratatouille

Yield: 4 portions

Category: Main

Cuisine: French; vegetarian

Source: Kochvergnuegen p. 185

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef tomatoes
  • 500 g aubergines
  • salt
  • 3 peppers (red, green, yellow)
  • 500 g courgettes
  • 2 red chillies
  • 250 g onions
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 100 ml olive oil
  • 1 sprig rosemary
  • pepper
  • French bread

Instructions

Pour boiling water over the tomatoes and skin them. Then cut them into quarters and remove all liquid parts and seeds, keeping only the firm flesh. Cut that into smaller pieces.

Cut the aubergines into 1cm thick slices, then into cubes. Sprinkle with salt and set aside.

Cut the peppers and the courgettes into a pieces. Remove seeds from chillies and cut finely. Cut the onions into thin slices.

Rinse the aubergines cubes under running water, then dry them with kitchen paper.

Heat the olive oil in a very large pot and sautee onions and crushed garlic for 5 minutes. Add peppers and sautee for 5 minutes more. Then add aubergines, courgettes, chillies, and tomatoes. Season well with salt and pepper. Add the rosemary twig at the top, cover, and simmer for 40 minutes.

Remove all rosemary needles. Check the seasoning.

Serve with with fresh French bread.
