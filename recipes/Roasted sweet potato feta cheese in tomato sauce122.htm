Title: Roasted sweet potato, feta cheese, in tomato sauce

Yield: 3 portions

Cooking time: 35 minutes

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Mediteranean

Source: Ottolenghi (Guardian)

Ingredients

  • 600 g sweet potato
  • 6 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • black pepper
  • 8 garlic gloves
  • 500 ml tomato passata
  • 1 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 250 g cherry tomatoes
  • 4 tbsp coriander leaves; roughly chopped
  • 200 g feta cheese

Instructions

Preheat oven to 220 deg.

Start rice or cous cous.

Peel potatoes, cut into large cubes, mix with salt, pepper, and 3 tbsps of olive oil. Place on a baking sheet and put into hot oven. Roast for 20 minutes.

Meanwhile, make the sauce. Heat the remaining two tablespoons of oil in a medium-sized, ovenproof frying pan on a medium-high heat. Once hot, add the garlic and cook, stirring occasionally, for about two minutes, until lightly golden. Turn down the heat to medium, then add the passata, sugar, whole tomatoes, some water, half a teaspoon of salt and a good grind of pepper. Cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce has thickened slightly and the tomatoes have softened.

Stir through the coriander, then remove the pan from the heat and add the sweet potato cubes. Top with the feta. Carefully wipe the rim of the pan (or it will burn) and grill for 8 minutes, or until the feta has taken on some colour. Top with the extra coriander and serve.
