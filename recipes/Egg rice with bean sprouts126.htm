Title: Egg rice with bean sprouts

Yield: 2 portions

Cooking time: 5 minutes

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Chinese

Source: Chefkoch.de

Original Page from www.chefkoch.de

Ingredients

  • Rice:
    • 4 cups rice from previous day or freshly cooked (from one cup of rice)
  • Omlette:
    • 2 eggs
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • pepper
    • 1 tbsp butter
  • Veggies:
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1 onion, thinly sliced
    • 1 green or red pepper
    • 2 tbsp cooking oil
    • 2 spring onions, or 1 thin stalk of leek, thinly sliced
    • 1 cup bean sprouts
    • 1 tbsp light soy sauce
    • pepper
  • 1/2 tsp sesame oil (optional)

Instructions

Start mung bean sprouts 4 days before you want to cook this. Then, on the day:

Prepare rice unless you have enough left-over in your fridge.

Mix ingredients for the omlette and pour the mixture into a hot pan with the melted butter.

Thinly slice onions, leek/spring onions, and bell pepper. Peel garlic cloves.

Heat a large pan, or a wok, and add cooking oil. Fry crushed garlic, onions, leek (if using), and pepper for about 2 minutes. Add rice, soy sauce, and freshly ground black pepper, and fry 2 minutes more. Add bean sprouts, spring onions (if using), and omlette cut into strips, and fry a final 1 minute. Check for seasoning.

Add a bit of sesame oil (if desired).

Serve immediately.
