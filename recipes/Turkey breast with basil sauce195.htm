Title: Turkey breast with basil sauce

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: German

Rating: 4.0/5 stars

Source: Brigitte Di√§t, p. 95

Ingredients

  • cooking oil
  • 600 g turkey breast; whole or as steaks
  • salt
  • paprika
  • 2 tbsp chicken broth
  • 16 basil leaves
  • 4 tsp capers
  • 10 tsp creme fraiche
  • 400 g delicate egg pasta, such as linguine

Instructions

Brush the turkey breasts on both sides with oil, then sprinkle with salt and paprika.

Heat more cooking oil in a casserole and fry the breasts on both sides. Reduce heat, add broth, and cover to allow to cook for 10 minutes.

Meanwhile, chop the basil leaves with the capers, and mix with the creme fraiche.

Remove turkey breast from casserole and slice into strips. Place in a serving dish and pour over the creme fraiche mixture.

Meanwhile cook the pasta, drain, and allow to soak up the juices in the casserole in which the turkey breasts where fried.
