Title: Non-meat hamburgers

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Vegetarian main

Cuisine: German

Source: Chefkoch.de

Original Page from www.chefkoch.de

Ingredients

  • For the rice:
    • vegetable broth
    • 150 g rice; sticky variety rather than Basmati
  • Other ingredients:
    • 2 eggs
    • 2 carots; grated
    • 150 g Emmentaler, grated
    • 2 onions; finely diced
    • 4 tbsp bread crumbs
    • plenty of pepper
    • 4 tbsp chopped herbs; parsley is essential
    • salt
  • For dusting the patties:
    • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • For the frying:
    • sunflower oil

Instructions

Cook the rice in vegetable broth using your preferred method.

Grate cheese and carots. Finely dice onions.

Mix all ingredients together (use plenty of pepper). Leave to bond for 15 minutes.

Form about 15 patties and roll them in more breadcrumbs.

Fry them golden brown on both sides in plenty of sunflower oil. Don't overheat your pan.

Put them on kitchen towels to remove some of the oil. Voila!
