Title: Lentil Soup (traditional)

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Category: Soup

Cuisine: German

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Ingredients

  • Vegetables:
    • 1 onion (about 80g)
    • 2 stalks celery
    • 100 g lardons (optional)
    • 1 large potato (about 150g)
    • 2 carots (about 100g)
    • 4 tbsp oil
  • Lentils:
    • 250 g lentils
    • 1 1/2 litres broth
    • 1 bay leaf
  • Flavourings:
    • 1 tsp dijon mustard
    • 100 ml red wine
    • seasoning
  • 8 hot dogs (in brine), or some other sausages (optional)

Instructions

Prepare the vegetables, cutting all of them into small cubes.

Heat about 4 tbsp of oil in the pressure cooker, then saut√© the onions and the celery (with the lardons, if using). Add the carrot and potato cubes and fry some more.

Now add the lentils, broth and bay leaf. Close the pressure cooker and cook for 15 mins.

After the pressure has gone back down and you can open it, add the mustard and wine, and season to taste.

If using, allow the hot dogs to warm up in the soup (you can add the brine from the hot dogs to the soup).
