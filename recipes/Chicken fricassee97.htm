Title: Chicken fricassee

Yield: 4 portions

Category: Main

Cuisine: German

Source: Unser Kochbuch No 1, p. 214

Ingredients

  • Chicken:
    • 1 whole chicken; about 1000g
    • root vegetables
    • salt
    • pepper corns
    • bay leaf
    • 1 onion
  • gravy:
    • 40 g butter
    • 40 g flour
    • 200 g asparagus; canned
    • 200 g mushrooms
    • 2 egg yolks
    • 125 ml cream
    • pepper
    • 1 tsp lemon juice
    • 3 tbsp white wine
    • 1 tbsp capers
  • 100 g ox tongue (optional)
  • puff pastry shells

Instructions

Cut the root vegetables and the onion into pieces. Put everything into a large pot together with the chicken and spices, and cover with water. Bring to a boil and simmer at low heat for 1 hour.

Leave to cool. Remove all fat from the surface. Bone the chicken and cut all meat into small cubes.

Cut the mushrooms into cubes of 1cm length. Sautee in butter until soft.

Drain the asparagus, keeping the water, and cut into a 3cm pieces.

Melt the butter and slightly cook the flour in it. Add chicken broth one spoonful at a time, stirring all the while. Continue until you have a thick gravy.

Add everything into the gravy: chicken meat, mushrooms, asparagus, cream, egg yolks, wine, lemon juice, capers.

Check for seasoning.

Serve with puff pastry shells.
