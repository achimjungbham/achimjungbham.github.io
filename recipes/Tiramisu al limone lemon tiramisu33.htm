Title: Tiramisu al limone (lemon tiramisu)

Yield: 4 servings

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Dessert

Cuisine: Italian

Source: Frau Buettner

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whipping cream
  • 300 g mascarpone
  • 150 g sugar; to taste
  • 30 ml Limoncello
  • 1 1/2 can peeled grapefruit
  • 1 1/2 pkg sponge fingers
  • lemon peel

Instructions

Whip cream. Add sugar to mascarpone and stir well. Add some limoncello and lemon zest. Add the whipped cream. Check for sweetness.

Put sponge fingers into a casserole dish one layer deep.

Mix some of the grapefruit liquid with limoncello and pour over sponge fingers, making sure they don't become soggy.

Put a layer of small cut grapefruit pieces over the sponge fingers, then cover with the mascarpone-cream mixture.

Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.
