Title: Sauerkraut (mit Gnocchi)

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: German

Source: Das neue Kochvergnuegen

Ingredients

  • 2 onions; thinly sliced
  • butter
  • 300 g Sauerkraut
  • 200 g cooked ham
  • 150 g creme fraiche
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  • black pepper
  • nutmeg
  • salt

Instructions

Saute onion rings in butter until soft. Add sauerkraut and ham. Gently cook for at least 20 minutes.

Mix creme fraiche, spices and salt, and add to the kraut. Cook for a little longer.

Notes

Goes well with gnocchi.
