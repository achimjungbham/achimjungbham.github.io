Title: Sauerkraut and Gnocchi

Yield: 2 servings

Cooking time: 1 hour

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: German

Source: Das neue Kochvergnuegen

Ingredients

  • 1 onion; thinly sliced
  • sunflower oil and/or butter
  • 300 g sauerkraut
  • 2 sprigs of fresh thyme or 1/2 tsp dried
  • 5 juniper berries
  • black pepper
  • nutmeg
  • 1/3 tsp salt
  • 200 g cooked ham
  • 150 g creme fraiche
  • 200 g gnocchi

Instructions

Thinly slice the onion. Rinse the sauerkraut under running water.

Saute onion rings until soft. Add herbs, spices and sauerkraut. Put on a lid and gently cook for 40 minutes.

Mix in crème fraîche and ham and cook for 10 more minutes.

Meanwhile boil the gnocchi for 2 minutes in saltwater, drain, and fry in oil and/or butter. Salt slightly.
