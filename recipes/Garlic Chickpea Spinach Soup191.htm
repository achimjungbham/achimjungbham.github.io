Title: Garlic Chickpea Spinach Soup

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 25 minutes

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Category: Soup

Cuisine: Indian; vegetarian

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Vegetarian; p. 175

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic; crushed
  • 1 large onion; roughly chopped
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cans chickpeas
  • 200 g sour cream
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 250 g spinach
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • cayenne pepper
  • salt
