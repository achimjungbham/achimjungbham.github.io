Title: Omlette Curry

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 40 minutes

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Indian; vegetarian

Source: 50 great curries of India

Ingredients

  • For the omlette
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 small onion; finely chopped
  • 2 green chillies; finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon coriander leaves; finely chopped
  • 1 pinch ground pepper
  • salt
  • oil
  • For the curry
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 2 teaspoons coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 3 small onions; thinly sliced
  • 2-3 green chillies; finely chopped
  • 2 cans chopped tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons coconut fat
  • 1 tablespoon coriander leaves
  • salt

Instructions

To make the omelette, whisk the eggs. Add the onion, chillies, coriander leaves, salt and pepper to taste and mix well. Heat a little oil in a large pan and, when hot, pour in the egg mixture. When the lower side of the omelette is cooked, turn over and let the other side cook, then remove from the heat. Cut the omelette into small 2x2cm pieces and set aside.

To make the curry, heat the oil and in a medium pot and fry the onions on low heat for 10 minutes. Add the spices and fry for a short while. Add tomatoes, chillies and vinegar. Add perhaps a cup of water. Salt to taste. Simmer for 10 minutes.

Add coconut fat and mix well. Add omelette pieces and coriander leaves and put on low heat for about 3 minutes.

Serve with rice.
