Title: Chicken Tikka

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Preparation time: 25 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Indian

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 tblsp yoghurt
  • 2 tblsp oil
  • 1 tblsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger
  • 1 clove fresh garlic
  • 2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 pinch saffron (optional)
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

Cut meat into bite-sized chunks.
Crush ginger and garlic and mix well with all other ingredients in a mixing bowl (with a lid).
Add meat and marinate for 1 day.

Next day:
Preheat oven to 200C.
Brush a baking sheet with oil and place meat pieces on it.
Cook for about 15 minutes.

Notes

Nice with basmati rice and salad.

Nice next day, too, as a sandwich filler.
