Title: Salmon skewers

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Main

Cuisine: English

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: BBC Good Food

Original Page from www.bbc.co.uk

Ingredients

  • 100 ml soy sauce
  • 100 g sugar
  • 40 ml mirin
  • 30 ml sake (or vodka)
  • 6 spring onions, finely chopped
  • 2 1/2 cm ginger, finely chopped
  • 600 g salmon filet

Instructions

Mix the sauce ingredients together in a saucepan, bring to a boil, and let simmer for about 15 minutes. You want the sauce to be sticky. Strain it through a sieve to get your glazing.

Cut the salmon filet into chunks, thread them onto wooden skewers, and brush the glaze on.

Grill for about 3-4 minutes or until the salmon is cooked through. Baste the hot skewers with any remaining glaze.

Serve with rice and salad.
