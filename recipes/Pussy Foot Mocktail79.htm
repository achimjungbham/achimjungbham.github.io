Title: Pussy Foot (Mocktail)

Yield: 4 Cocktails

Preparation time: 10 minutes

Category: Drinks

Source: Tanis

Ingredients

  • 150 ml pink grapefruit juice
  • 150 ml orange juice
  • 1/2 tsp lime juice
  • 20 ml Grenadine
  • ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

Mix orange, grapefruit and lime juice. Pour into cocktail glasses. Carefully add the Grenadine so that it creates a coloured bottom layer.
