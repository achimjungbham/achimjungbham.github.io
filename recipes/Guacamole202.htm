Title: Guacamole

Yield: 4 portions

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Dip

Cuisine: Mexican

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Ingredients

  • 1 lime; juiced
  • 2 medium avocados; or 1 large one
  • 1 bunch coriander
  • 1 green chilli; de-seeded and finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • pepper
  • 2 large tomatoes
  • 3 spring onions

Instructions

Juice the lime and pour in a narrow but high bowl. Add the flesh of the avocados, the coriander, the chilli, half a tsp of salt and a good grind of pepper. Blitz with the hand blender until you get your preferred consistency. Check the seasoning.

Pour boiling water over the tomatoes, then peel them, cut into quarters and remove all seeds and the watery flesh. Cut the remaining firm flesh into fine cubes and combine with the avocado mash.

Very finely cut the spring onions and add them, too. Alternatively, blitz them together with the avocado flesh.
