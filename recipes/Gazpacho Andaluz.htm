Title: Gazpacho Andaluz

Yield: 1.5 litre

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Cooking time: none

Category: Soup

Cuisine: Spanish; vegetarian

Source: Chefkoch.de

Ingredients

  • Soup
    • 6 medium tomatoes
    • 1 red pepper
    • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
    • 1 cup bread crumbs
    • 1 can chopped tomatoes
    • 1.5 tsp salt (to taste)
    • 1 tsp mild chilli powder (optional)
    • 5 tbsp olive oil
    • 5 tbsp vinegar
  • Side
    • French bread
    • salted butter

Instructions

Prepare the pepper and tomatoes and cut into chunks. Put all ingredients into a blender (possibly in two batches) and blend thoroughly. Put in a serving bowl and chill.

Serve with French bread and salted butter.
