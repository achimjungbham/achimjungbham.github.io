Title: Salmon in Foil

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 25 minutes

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: French

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Ingredients

  • 500 g salmon filet (with skin)
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 8 leaves basil
  • 2 medium courgettes
  • 4 medium tomatoes
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 800 g baby potatoes

Instructions

Steam the potatoes in the pressure cooker, skin on.

Pre-heat the oven to 200 deg Celsius

De-seed the tomatoes and cut into small cubes (1.5 cm edge). Cut the courgettes into similarly sized cubes. Mix with salt and pepper.

Cut four wedges from the lemon.

Cut off four good sized sheets of aliminium foil. In the center of each, lay a piece of salmon filet, skin down. Lightly salt and pepper. Garnish with basil leaves and lemon wedge. Surround with tomato and courgette cubes. Fold up the foil to make an air-tight parcel.

Place the parcels on a baking sheet and bake for 25 minutes in the hot oven.
