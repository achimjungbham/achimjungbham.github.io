Title: Risotto alla Milanese

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Preparation time: 5 minutes

Category: Starter

Cuisine: Italian; can be vegetarian

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Kochvergnuegen p. 220

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 250 g risotto rice
  • 750 ml strong broth
  • saffron
  • 50 g parmesan, finely grated
  • salt
  • pepper
  • more butter

Instructions

Prepare the broth, either meat or vegetarian. Finely grate the parmesan. Chop the onion.

Melt half the butter in a pan and saut√© the onion until translucent. Add the rice and gently cook for a while.

Now add the broth one spoonful at a time, allowing it to be absorbed before adding the next one. Stir constantly.

After about 15 minutes add the remaining broth and the saffron in one go and cook for a further 5 minutes.

Add the parmesan and season to taste. Add more butter if desired.
