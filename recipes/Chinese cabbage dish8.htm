Title: Chinese cabbage dish

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Main

Cuisine: Chinese

Source: Brigitte Diaet

Ingredients

  • 400 g Quorn mince; or minced beef
  • oil
  • chinese five spice
  • 2 stalks leek; cut into thin slices
  • 500 g white cabbage; finely shredded
  • 3 carrots; finely shredded
  • 1 piece ginger; crushed
  • 3 clove garlic; crushed
  • 100 ml vegetable broth; instant
  • 7 tblsp soy sauce
  • pepper
  • cayenne pepper (optional)
  • 1 tblsp lemon juice (optional)
  • fresh coriander; chopped (optional)

Instructions

Prepare all the vegetables, garlic and ginger. Prepare the (instant) vegetable broth.

Heat oil in large pan, fry mince until crumbly and well browned. Take out, sprinkle with Chinese five spice and and cover.

Add more oil to the same pan, and stir fry the vegetables. Add garlic, ginger, broth and soy sauce. Cover and cook for a few minutes, making sure you don't overcook!

Add mince to vegetables, mix well. Flavour with pepper and a pinch of cayenne pepper. (Extra salt will not be needed.) Spritz with some lemon juice if available. Put everything into a serving dish and sprinkle with the chopped coriander leaves.

Notes

Serve with rice.
