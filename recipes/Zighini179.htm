Title: Zighini

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 45 minutes

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Eritrean

Source: Paola

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 4 medium onions
  • 500 g chicken (with or without bones); or vegan alternative
  • 2 tbsp Berbère
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • Accompaniments:
    • 4 eggs
    • rice for 4 persons
    • green salad

Instructions

Heat ghee and oil in a suitably large casserole. Chop the onions. Fry them until they start to soften. Put them in a small high bowl and blitz with a hand blender. Return to the casserole.

Add the chopped meat and berbère and cook for a few minutes. If you are using vegan meat, then it may be advisable to add some chicken stock cube at this point.

Add canned tomatoes, mix well, cover, and let simmer for a minimum of 30 minutes.

In the meantime, boil the eggs for 10 minutes, start a pot of rice, and prepare some salad to go with the meal.

When the cooking time is up, add the hard-boiled eggs and serve with rice and salad.
