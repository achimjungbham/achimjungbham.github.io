Title: Baba Ganoush

Yield: 2/5 kg

Cooking time: 40 minutes

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Category: Starter

Cuisine: Middle Eastern; vegetarian

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: BBC Good Food

Ingredients

  • 2 aubergines
  • 40 ml olive oil
  • 1/4 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 1 large lemon; juiced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 bunch parsley; finely chopped
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

Heat grill/oven to highest setting. Put aubergines on a baking sheet and grill for 35-40 minutes, or until completely softened, blackened, and blistered, turning them halfway through with a pair of tongs. Set aside and leave to cool completely.

Once aubergines are cool enough to handle, gently peel off the charred skin and discard. Transfer the flesh to the bowl of a blender along with 30ml of the olive oil, the coriander, cumin, lemon juice and half the parsley. Season and pulse the flesh three or four times to create a chunky textured dip.

Transfer the dip to a serving bowl and drizzle with the remaining oil, scatter over any leftover parsley and serve with warm flatbreads or pita.
