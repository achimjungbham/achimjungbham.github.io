Title: Serbian Bean Stew (Serbische Bohnensuppe)

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Soup

Cuisine: European

Source: Graefe Unzer: Unser Kochbuch, p.51

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 200 g pancetta
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 can choppped tomatoes
  • 2 cans butter beans
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3/4 litre broth
  • 1 tsp majoram
  • 1 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 3 Tbs sour cream
  • 2 Tbs red wine
  • pepper
  • salt

Instructions

Heat the olive oil in a large pot. Add the chopped onions, garlic and pancetta (cubes or strips). Gently fry while chopping the two peppers. Add those and continue to fry everything (but don't let it become burned).

Add the beans (together with their liquid) and the tomatoes. Add all the spices. Add the broth and bring to a boil. Simmer for about 10 minutes or however long you want to enjoy the good smells.

Add sour cream and red wine. Season to taste.
