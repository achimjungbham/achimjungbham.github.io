Title: Risotto ai funghi

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 20 minutes

Preparation time: 35 minutes

Category: Starter

Cuisine: Italian; can be vegetarian

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Chefkoch.de

Ingredients

  • 20 g dried porcini
  • olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 300 g mushrooms, sliced
  • 200 g risotto rice
  • 1 tbsp fresh thyme
  • 100 ml white wine
  • 750 ml strong vegetable broth
  • 2 heads pak choi (opt)
  • 25 g butter
  • 40 g parmesan, finely grated
  • pepper
  • nutmeg

Instructions

Soak the porcini in cold water for 30 minutes.

Prepare the broth, finely grate the parmesan. Chop the onion.

Saut√© the onion in the olive oil until translucent. Add the mushrooms, the drained and chopped porcini, and the thyme, and cook for 3 minutes. Add the rice and gently cook for another 3 minutes. Now add the wine and allow it to be absorbed.

Add the broth one spoonful at a time, allowing it to be absorbed before adding the next one. Stir constantly.

After about 15 minutes add the remaining broth, the pak choi (if using) and cook for a further 5 minutes. Check whether the rice is to your liking. You can add more water and cook for longer.

Add the butter, parmesan, and spices (salt won't be needed). Serve asap.
