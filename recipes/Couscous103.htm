Title: Couscous

Yield: 4 portions

Category: Side

Cuisine: Middle Eastern

Ingredients

  • 240 g couscous
  • 450 ml vegetable stock
  • butter

Instructions

Mix the couscous with the hot vegetable stock until it begins to thicken. Cover and let stand until all the moisture is absorbed (about 10 minutes). Add a few pieces of butter on top and let melt. Loosen with a fork before serving.
