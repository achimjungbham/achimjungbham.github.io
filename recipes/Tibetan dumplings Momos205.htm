Title: Tibetan dumplings (Momos)

Yield: 20 dumplings

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Tibetan

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Taste Tibet, p.153

Ingredients

  • Dough:
    • 250 g self-raising flour
    • 115 ml water
  • Vegan momos:
    • 280 g pointed cabbage
    • 100 g spinach
    • 100 g chives
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tbsp cooking oil
  • Mushroom momos:
    • 125 g chestnut mushrooms
    • 100 g pak choi
    • 100 g pointed cabbage
    • 2 spring onions
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tbsp cooking oil
    • 1/2 tsp coriander seeds
    • 120 g firm tofu
  • Beef momos:
    • 1 red onion
    • 1 large garlic clove
    • 250 g minced beef
    • 50 g spinach
    • 1 tsp cooking oil
    • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

Mix water and flour and knead well. Leave to stand for 20 minutes.

Meanwhile finely chop your chosen fillings.

Knead dough some more, divide into 20 equal pieces.

For each dumpling roll out thinly to about 12cm diameter, place a heaped tablespoon of filling in the middle of it and gather the rim together so that no opening remains.

Steam for 15 minutes over high heat, or add to a soup and simmer for 7 minutes.

Steamed momos can be fried in butter.
