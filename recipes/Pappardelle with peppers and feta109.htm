Title: Pappardelle with peppers and feta

Yield: 4 portions

Category: Main

Cuisine: German; vegetarian

Source: 1 Nudel - 50 Saucen, p. 25

Ingredients

  • 3 small peppers; red, green, yellow
  • 300 g tomatoes
  • 2 spring onions
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 4 sprigs thyme
  • 4 leaves sage
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 100 g feta

Instructions

Wash the peppers and cut into very small cubes. Cut the tomatoes likewise. Cut the spring onion into thin rings. Chop thyme and sage leaves. Peel the garlic.

Heat oil in a pan (for which you have a lid) and sautee onions, crushed garlic, and herbs. Add pepper cubes and sautee some more. Now add tomato pieces and season with salt, pepper, and paprika. Cover with the lid and let simmer for 8 minutes.

Cook your pasta and drain and rinse. Mix with the pepper sauce. Crumble the feta cheese on top.
