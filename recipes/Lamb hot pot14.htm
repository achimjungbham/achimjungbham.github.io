Title: Lamb hot pot

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 1 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 45 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Spanish

Source: Internet

Ingredients

  • 600 g new potatoes
  • 600 g lamb; diced
  • olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 red pepper; diced
  • 5 sprigs thyme
  • 250 g chorizo; sliced
  • 4 olive; chopped
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes

Instructions

Boil new potatoes.

In the meantime, fry lamb pieces. Add garlic, onion, and pepper and fry until soft.

Add chopped chorizo, diced olives, chopped tomatoes, peeled potatoes, and thyme. Add a bit of water if necessary.

Bring to a boil and let simmer for 90 minutes.
