Title: Pichelsteiner

Yield: 2 portions

Category: Main

Cuisine: German

Source: Siebeck p. 300

Ingredients

  • 300 g fillet steak
  • 100 g butter
  • 1 large carot
  • 1/4 celeriac
  • 1 leek
  • 2 salad potatoes
  • 1 bay leaf
  • pepper
  • salt
  • allspice

Instructions

Get all the vegetables ready: Slice the carot very thinly; slice the potatoes somewhat thicker; thicker still the celeriac and the leek.

Cut the fillet steaks into pieces, between 1/2 and 1cm thick. Salt lightly.

In a large pan heat the butter, add the vegetables and the bay leaf, and season with salt, pepper, and allspice. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Make room in the middle of the pan for the meat and fry for a short while, then pour everything into a pre-heated large pot. Cover the pot and stew for 10 minutes.
