Title: Chorizo Chestnut Soup

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 35 minutes

Preparation time: 25 minutes

Category: Soup

Cuisine: Spanish

Source: Moro (Tanis and James; 2013-12-26)

Ingredients

  • 4 tblsp Olive oil
  • 1 large Spanish onion; diced
  • 1 medium carrot; diced
  • 1 stick celery; diced
  • 120 grams mild cooking chorizo; cut into 1cm cubes
  • 2 cloves garlic; thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 1/2 tsp fresh thyme; chopped
  • 2 small dried red chillies; diced
  • 1 can tomatoes; chopped
  • 500 grams chestnuts; cooked peeled roughly chopped
  • 20 strands saffron; infused in 3-4 tblsp boiling water
  • 1 l water

Instructions

Sweat the oil, onion, carrot, celery and chorizo with a pinch of salt for 20 minutes.

Add the garlic, cumin, thyme, and chilli. Cook for 1 minute.

Add tomatoes, cook for 2 minutes.

Add the chestnuts, stir. Add saffron liquid and the water. Simmer for 10 minutes.

Remove from heat and mash with potato masher but keep some of the texture of the chestnuts.
