Title: Yeast dough (sweet)

Category: Cakes

Cuisine: German

Source: Louise Haarer, p. 169

Ingredients

  • Pre-dough
    • 230 ml milk
    • 1 tsp dried yeast
    • 200 g flour
  • Main dough
    • 60-80 g sugar
    • 1 pinch salt
    • 1 large egg
    • zest from 1 lemon (opt.)
    • approx. 300 g flour
  • Fat
    • 80-100 g butter

Instructions

Heat the milk so that it has a temperature of about 30℃. If the milk comes from the fridge then 40 seconds in the microwave oven will work nicely. Dissolve the yeast in the milk, add a pinch of sugar and the flour. Mix well and allow to ferment for 40-60 minutes in a warm place.

Add the sugar, salt, egg, and zest to the pre-dough and mix well so that the sugar can dissolve. Add the flour and work into a nice dough. Leave to proof for at least an hour. It should have risen nicely by this time.

Work the soft butter into the dough, adding a bit more flour if it gets too sticky again.

You can now line a cake tin or a baking sheet with the appropriate amount of dough. Leave it to rise for another 30 minutes before you add toppings etc.
