Title: Potato pancakes (Grumbeerpannekuche)

Yield: 24 pancakes

Cooking time: 1/2 hours

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Category: Complete meal

Cuisine: German; vegetarian

Source: Oma Irmgard

Ingredients

  • 1 kg potatoes
  • 1 small onion
  • 3 eggs
  • salt
  • nutmeg
  • 1 pinch allspice
  • 3 tblsp rolled oats
  • oil; plenty!
  • 400 g apple compote

Instructions

Peel potatoes and shred finely. Salt slightly and leave so that the potatoes can shed some of their water. Chop onion very finely. Drain potatoes and mix everything together with other ingredients.

Fry small pancakes in pan with plenty of oil on both sides. Note that this can take a while, so a second pan is advisable.

Keep fried pancakes in warm oven until all are done.

Serve with apple compote.
