Title: Lemon posset

Yield: 6 servings

Cooking time: 10 minutes

Preparation time: 5 minutes

Category: Dessert

Cuisine: American

Source: Guardian

Ingredients

  • 600 ml double cream
  • 150 g sugar
  • 1 tblsp vanilla sugar (optional)
  • 3 lemons

Instructions

Heat cream and sugar in a large saucepan, stirring to dissolve the sugar.

Let boil for exactly 3 minutes, without stirring.

Remove from heat and add the lemon juice.

Pour into six ramekins.

Cool, cover, and refrigerate for at least four hours before serving.
