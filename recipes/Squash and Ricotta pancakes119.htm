Title: Squash and Ricotta pancakes

Yield: 8 pancakes

Cooking time: 40 minutes

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Category: Main

Cuisine: Contemporary English; vegetarian

Source: Guardian

Ingredients

  • 300 g butternut squash
  • 3 eggs
  • 175 g ricotta cheese
  • 5 tbsp coriander leaves; chopped
  • 2 spring onions; finely chopped
  • 1/2 lemon zest
  • 1/2 red chilli; deseeded and finely chopped
  • seasoning
  • 100 g plain flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • flour; for dusting
  • cooking oil
  • salad

Instructions

Preheat oven to 200 deg Celsius.

Cut butternut squash into largish cubes. Put on greased baking sheet and roast for 30 minutes or until very soft.

Mix eggs, ricotta, herbs, zest, chilli, and add seasoning to taste (about 1/2 tsp salt and pepper each).

Add cooked squash cubes, flour, and baking powder. Mix well.

Heat oil in pan.

The mixture will be quite sticky, so measure out spoonfuls of the pancake dough and let them drop into a bowl of flower. Form about 8-10 flour-dusted patties.

Fry from both sides for about 3 minutes each.

Serve with salad.
