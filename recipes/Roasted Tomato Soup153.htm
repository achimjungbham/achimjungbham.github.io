Title: Roasted Tomato Soup

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 2 hours

Preparation time: 5 minutes

Category: Soup

Cuisine: Irish

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Source: Cafe Paradiso, p. 58

Ingredients

  • Roast:
    • 2 kg ripe tomatoes
    • 8 cloves garlic
  • Soup base:
    • 1 sprig rosemary
    • 1 sprig thyme
    • 2 onions
    • 4 tbsp olive oil
    • 100 ml tomato passata
    • 800 ml stock
    • seasoning
  • Garnish:
    • 1 cup fresh herbs
  • Sides:
    • crusty rolls
    • butter

Instructions

Pre-heat oven to 200 deg fan.

Wash the tomatoes and remove the cores. Cut in two along the equator and place skin down on a baking sheet. Peel the garlic cloves and add to the tomatoes. Put in oven for between 1 hour and 90 minutes. Take out when the skin is starting to get burned.

In the meantime, chop the onions and saut√© in olive oil in a large saucepan until translucent. Add the passata, the stock, rosemary, thyme and pepper. Cook on low heat for about 15 min, then wait for the tomatoes to be ready. Once they are, peel them (optional) and add them to the soup. Add the garlic as well.

Now take out the two herb sprigs and use the hand blender to make a smooth soup. Check for seasoning and finally add the chopped fresh herbs (basil, marjoram, parsley, chives, whatever is to hand).

Serve with crusty white bread and butter.
