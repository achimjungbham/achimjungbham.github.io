Title: Vanilla custard

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 5 minutes

Preparation time: 5 minutes

Category: Sauce

Cuisine: German

Rating: 4.0/5 stars

Source: Louise Harrer, p.127

Ingredients

  • 1/2 l milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 1/2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 sachet vanilla sugar
  • 1 tbsp cornflour

Instructions

Mix all ingredients well together and pour into a cooking pot. Keep stirring all the while it heats up. Once it boils take off heat immediately. Let cool before using.
