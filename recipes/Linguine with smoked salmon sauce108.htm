Title: Linguine with smoked salmon sauce

Yield: 2 portions

Category: Main

Cuisine: German

Source: 1 Nudel - 50 Saucen, p. 9

Ingredients

  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 stalk sellerie
  • 1 spring onion
  • 4 sprigs basil
  • 100 g smoked salmon
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 75 creme fraiche
  • 75 yogurt
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 220 g pasta, eg linguine

Instructions

Chop very finely the pepper, the celery, the basil leaves, and the spring onion.

Cut the salmon into small strips.

Wash the lemon and grate the peel. Squeeze for the juice, 3 tsp are needed.

Mix everything together and season well. Keep in the fridge until the pasta is ready.
