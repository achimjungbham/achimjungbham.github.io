Title: Rich sponge fruit cake

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Baking time: 15 minutes

Category: Cake

Ingredients

  • Sponge
    • 3 eggs, separated
    • 150 g caster sugar
    • 50 g brown sugar
    • 75ml milk
    • 115 g wholemeal flour
    • 2 tsp cinnamon powder
    • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • Topping
    • fresh soft fruit, eg raspberries or blackberries
    • 300ml whipping cream
    • sugar to taste

Instructions

Preheat oven to 200℃. Line a sponge cake tin with baking paper at the bottom and grease the rim well.

Whip the egg whites until stiff, then add egg yolks, milk, and sugars. Whisk until well combined.

Mix cinnamon and baking powder into the flour, then fold into the egg mixture.

Pour into the tin and bake for about 15 minutes. Be careful that it doesn't burn at the top. When it is ready, take out of the oven and wait a few minutes until the cake has withdrawn from the rim of the tin. Then turn over onto a cooling grid and allow to fully cool.

Whip the cream and add sugar to taste, then add the fruit. Spread the mixture onto the sponge base. Voilá!
