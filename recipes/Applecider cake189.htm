Title: Apple-cider cake

Yield: 1 cake

Category: Cake

Cuisine: German

Source: Heidi Conrad

Ingredients

  • Base:
    • 200 g flour (or up to 250g)
    • 100 g sugar
    • 125 g butter
    • 1 egg
    • 2 tsp baking powder
  • Filling:
    • 1 kg apples (Cox orange work well)
    • 500 ml cider
    • 250 g sugar
    • 2 packs vanilla custard powder (Vanillepudding)
    • 250 ml whipping cream

Instructions

Prepare the shortcrust dough in your favourite way, for example, mix butter, sugar, flour, and baking powder with a hand mixer until you get a consistency of corn meal. Than add the egg and possibly some more cold water to bind it all together. Do not knead. Wrap in cling film and leave in the fridge for at half an hour.

Meanwhile, peel and core the apples, then cut into 1cm cubes. Also, pre-heat the oven to 180 deg Celsius.

Line a 26cm baking tin with your dough. Fill it with your apple cubes.

Prepare a thick vanilla custard from the cider, sugar and custard powder, and pour over the apple cubes. Bake for 35-45 minutes.

Serve with plenty of whipped cream, or, if the cake will be eaten in a single sitting, then add a layer of whipped cream on top before serving.
