Title: Tomato galette

Yield: 4 servings

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Vegetarian main

Cuisine: Contemporary English; vegetarian

Source: Ottolenghi (Guardian)

Ingredients

  • 375 g puff pastry
  • 8 stalks fresh oregano; leaves picked and chopped
  • 100 g goat's cheese
  • 450 g tomatoes; of various sizes, sliced 3mm thick
  • 8 stalks fresh thyme
  • olive oil
  • Tomato paste:
    • 10 sun-dried tomatoes
    • 1 red chilli
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1/2 tsp sugar
    • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

Preheat oven to 200C.

Roll out pastry to 3mm thick and cut out four rectangles about 10x15cm. Transfer the pastry pieces to a baking sheet lined with baking paper and refrigerate (if you can).

Meanwhile, make the sun-dried tomato paste: put all the ingredients in a bowl and blend into a paste. If necessary, add a little oil from the tomato jar to make the paste smoother.

Spread a thin layer of the paste over the pastry, leaving a border of about 1cm around the edges.
Sprinkle with the oregano and goat's cheese.
Arrange the tomatoes on top, making sure the paste is completely covered (or it will dry out).
Drop the thyme stalks over the tomatoes and drizzle with a little olive oil.

Bake for 15 minutes until golden on top.

Remove from the oven and leave to cool somewhat before serving.
