Title: Potato Gratin (Freiburger Kartoffeln)

Yield: 4 portions

Cooking time: 1 hour

Preparation time: 1/2 hours

Category: Side

Cuisine: German; vegetarian

Rating: 5.0/5 stars

Ingredients

  • 750 g potatoes
  • 180 g gouda cheese
  • 250 ml light cream
  • 2 eggs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • nutmeg

Instructions

Boil the potatoes, then peel them and cut them into 0.5cm thick slices.

Grate the cheese.

Grease an oven-proof dish with butter, then layer the potatoes and the cheese alternately, starting and ending with a layer of potatoes.

Mix well cream and eggs. Season with salt, pepper and nutmeg to taste.

Heat oven to 175 deg Celsius and bake for about 1 hour.
